# Tayrona National Natural Park

Tayrona is one of the most beautiful places in Colombia, offering a spectacular strip of beaches next to a huge jungle with animals and indigenous villages. Details:
* Arrival: Direct bus, boat directly to the beach from Taganga, or taxi to park entrance combined with a 2.5-hour walk, or paid van.
* Main beaches: Arrecifes, La Piscina, Cabo San Juan (Cabo is the most beautiful).
* Sleeping in tents or hammocks on beaches (booking done at entrance).
* Entry payment by credit card possible, but bring cash for stay.
* Note (2023): Park closed for maintenance Feb 1-15, June 1-15, Oct 19-Nov 2.
