# Aguas Calientes

A lovely town at the foot of the most important site in the continent - Machu Picchu. The town serves as a transit station to the famous site and also offers famous hot springs after which the city is named Aguas Calientes (Hot Springs).

**Arrival:**
* Drive with van (dangerous, avoid this option) to Hidroelectrica station and from there walk about 2.5 hours on train tracks to Aguas Calientes.
* Train directly from Cusco.
* Taxi to Ollantaytambo train station (about 2.5 hours from Cusco) and from there another 1.5 hours by train (the safe option).
