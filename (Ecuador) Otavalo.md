# Otavalo

Colorful city in the north known as an authentic city attracting many tourists. You can explore:
* Famous Indian Market with hundreds of stalls (clothes, instruments, jewelry, leather; largest in South America on Saturdays).
* Condor Park.
* Socavon Cross tour.
* Volcano area trip.
* Cuicocha Lagoon walk (inside volcano crater, 12km loop).
* Walk to Peguche Waterfall (includes tunnel).
* San Pablo Lake trip.
* Mojanda Lagoons tour.
* Cotacachi and Imbabura volcanoes trip.
* Surrounding villages tours: Leather village, Wood village, Shaman village.
