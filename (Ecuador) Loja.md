# Loja

City in the south known for large vegetation in its area. Colorful city features:
* City Park Plaza.
* Podocarpus National Park.
* Beautiful ancient City Gate.
* Colorful Lourdes Street.
* Main Cathedral.
* El Valle (Old Quarter).
* Unique El Baia neighborhood.
