# Rio Dulce

The town itself is simple and doesn't offer too much but is adjacent to the pastoral river reserve area found in eastern Guatemala passing through Lakes Izabal and El Golfete, an area attracting many travelers and considered an impressive nature pearl.

**In this area you can do:**
* Trip to Finca El Paraíso
* El Boquerón area and hot water waterfalls
* Cruise to Livingston, the chill village on the Caribbean coast, and arrival at Playa Blanca beach
* Fishing in the lagoon and camping areas
* Visit to San Felipe fortress on the lake shore
* Jet skis
* Kayaking in the lake
* Trip to hot springs
* Tour of the Seven Altars reserve, adjacent to Livingston
* Trip to Sand Bay beach located north of Livingston
