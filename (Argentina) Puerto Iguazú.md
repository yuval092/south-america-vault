# Puerto Iguazú

A must-visit point in Argentina and a trip to South America. Arrival by bus or flight from Buenos Aires or Salta.

**Attractions:**
* The main attraction is of course the spectacular Iguazu Falls. The reserve itself is a kind of spectacular jungle and it is important to arrive early for those who want to hike in it beyond the falls.
* Sendero Macuco trek is 3.5 km long and leads to a waterfall
* Devil's Throat which is the drainage point of all waterfalls
* San Martin Island
* Night tour in the park and falls
* Jeep tour in the reserve
* Speedboat ride to the falls
* Waterfall rappelling
* The huge Itaipu Dam which is considered an especially impressive attraction

**Info:**
Open daily from 08:00 to 18:00, entry until 16:30.
