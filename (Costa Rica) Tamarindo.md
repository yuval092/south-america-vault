# Tamarindo

Non-stop vibrant surf town located in the northwest, visited by many travelers for its young character, nightlife, and spectacular beaches. Enjoy:
* Tamarindo Beach, Playa Grande.
* Jungle walks.
* Surf camps.
* Competitive fishing.
* Trip to Avellanas Beach.
* Horseback riding.
* ATV rental.
* Beach apartment rental.
* Kayaks.
* Surf shops and equipment rental.
* Jungle zip-line.
* Parties and bars.
* Monkey Park.
* Las Baulas National Park trip.
* Snorkeling.
