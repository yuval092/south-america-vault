# San Blas Islands

A group of islands isolated from communication offering a desert island experience, turquoise water and magical atmosphere. A must-visit point in Panama.

**Highlights:**
* You can sleep in cabins on the islands
* Visit local tribes

**Arrival:**
From the terminal in Panama City take a van to Carti town, get off at the beginning of the road leading to the town, there a shuttle will take you to the boat and from there to the islands. You can cross from Panama through San Blas Islands to Colombia.
