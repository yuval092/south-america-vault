# Santa Teresa

Beach town with warm weather located in northwest Costa Rica on the Pacific coast strip. Known for attractions:
* Surf camps and schools, diving, and snorkeling.
* Gliding.
* Spectacular surf beaches: Playa Carmen, Banana Beach, Santa Teresa Beach.
* Playa Mal Pais (10 min drive, small pools remain at low tide).
* ATV rental.
* Trip to Montezuma waterfalls.
* Horseback riding.
* Professional fishing competitions.
* Yoga classes.
* Bicycle tours.
* Tortuga Island tour.
* Canopy zip-lines.
* Kayaking.
* Watching some of the most beautiful sunsets in the continent.
* Boat to Jaco available with Zuma Tours.
