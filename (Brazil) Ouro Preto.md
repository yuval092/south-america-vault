# Ouro Preto

Considered by many as one of the most beautiful cities in Brazil. About 15,000 students live here, making it a tourist destination. Sites worth visiting:
* Saint Francis Church.
* Handicrafts and knick-knack market.
* Central square with cafes, restaurants, and street musicians.
* Guided gold mine tours.
* Student fraternity parties and changing festivals.
