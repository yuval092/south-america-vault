# Ilha Grande

One of the most beloved and popular islands in Brazil, offering spectacular beaches with lush green scenery; a must-visit for travelers. Highlights:
* Arrival from Rio via transfer combined with boat, or from SÃ£o Paulo via Angra dos Reis and then boat.
* Lopes Mendes Beach: Considered one of the 7 most beautiful beaches in the world (turquoise water, white sand; accessible via jungle trek).
* Lagoa Azul (Blue Lagoon): Destination for diving and snorkeling; amazing night diving available.
* Pico do Papagaio: Famous rock with a trek to the viewpoint.
* Trek to Cachoeira da Feiticeira waterfall through the jungle.
* Aventureiro Beach located in a biological reserve.
