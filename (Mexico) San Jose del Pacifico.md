# San Jose del Pacifico

A small village on the way from Oaxaca to Mazunte, located at an altitude of 2500 meters above the cloud line, giving it a particularly spectacular view. The village is known for:
* Its special mushrooms.
* Sweat lodge ceremony (Temazcal).
* Trek to ocean viewpoint.
* Walking on suspension bridge Bosque Encantado.
* Zip-line and lots of calm.
