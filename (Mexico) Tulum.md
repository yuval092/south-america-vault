# Tulum

A small city located about an hour west of Playa del Carmen along Mexico's Caribbean coast, some of the most beautiful in the continent. You can visit:
* The beautiful Playa Paraiso and the famous crooked tree.
* Trip to the Fisherman's Beach.
* Day tour of the Mayan ruins.
* Diving and snorkeling courses of all kinds.
* Sailing in Punta Laguna surrounded by rich vegetation and watching monkeys.
* Visit Ziggy Beach and restaurant complex.
* Zip-line in Cenote Labnaha.
* Diving experience in Cenote Dos Ojos.
* Walking on Mayan Beach and the beautiful viewpoint.
* Cave swimming in Gran Cenote.
* Trip to Tulum National Park.
* Cave snorkeling in Cenote Sac Actun.
* One of the big attractions in Tulum is the Zamna parties held throughout the year, the most famous being the Zamna Festival in late December and early January.
