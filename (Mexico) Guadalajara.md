# Guadalajara

The second largest city located in central Mexico and near the Pacific Ocean, known as a cultural symbol in the country. In the colorful and artistic city, you can visit:
* The main square - Plaza de Armas.
* The old town Tlaquepaque where you will find art shops and stalls.
* Especially impressive churches.
* Trip to the National Park.
* Visit to the Zoo.
* Bicycle riding and springs in Bosque de la Primavera (Spring Forest).
* The impressive Government Palace.
* Barranca de Huentitan nature reserve.
* Tour of the pastoral site Bosque Colomos.
* Sailing to Mezcala Island.
* Football games.
* Trip to Parque Independencia viewpoint.
* Tour of the spectacular Michin Aquarium.
* Visit to the huge Lake Chapala.
* Visit Lake Santa Maria del Oro - a round and perfect turquoise lagoon inside a volcano, camping possible.
* Visit the town of Tequila located half an hour drive away.
* Visit the cowboy town Tapalpa located about three hours from Guadalajara.
