# Barranquilla

The fourth-largest city in the country, lying along the Magdalena River, known as the 'Golden Gate'. Located in the north with warm weather year-round. Less interesting for tourists generally, but the Barranquilla Carnival is one of the biggest in the world. Points of interest:
* Puerta de Oro center.
* Plaza Intendencia Fluvial.
* Great Malecon Puerta de Oro.
* City Zoo.
* Walking on Bocas de Ceniza breakwater.
* Impressive Window of the World monument.
* Botanical Garden tour.
* Shopping at Buenavista and Portal del Prado malls, Viva Barranquilla mall.
* Tour of nearby Isla Salamanca Park.
