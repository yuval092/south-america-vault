# Quetzaltenango

The second largest city in Guatemala and sits at the edge of the Santa María Volcano. The city is at an altitude of 2300m and is usually cool. The city abounds with trips and attractions.

**Treks & Activities:**
* Trek to the lagoon and Chicabal Volcano
* Trek to the summit of the highest volcano in Central America (height 4220m, 7 hours climbing over two days, medium difficulty level)
* Trek to Santa María Volcano and watching lava eruption from the active Santiaguito Volcano
* 3-day trek to Lake Atitlán with accommodation in Maya villages and a route passing through mountains, corn fields, cloud forests and spectacular views
* Visit to Fuentes Georginas hot springs
* Visit to Cenotes de Candelaria and Hoyo Cimarrón
* Tour of romantic sites in the city
* Gastronomic tour
* Visit to Minerva Temple
* Visit to the huge Chichicastenango market
