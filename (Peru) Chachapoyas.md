# Chachapoyas

Pastoral town offering unique and rare places and attractions.

**Highlights:**
* Mummies Museum
* Quiocta Stalactite Cave
* Huaylla Belen National Park
* Sonche Canyon Trek
* Gocta - third highest waterfall in world
* Kuélap Fortress and cable car leading to site - considered the new Machu Picchu of Peru
* Sailing in Pomacochas Lagoon
* Laguna de los Cóndores
* Yumbilla Waterfalls
* Karajía Burial Site
* Huancas District and breathtaking views
* Levanto-Urco viewpoint
