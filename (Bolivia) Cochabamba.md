# Cochabamba

The third-largest city in Bolivia. Here you will find:
* The Amazon Garden.
* The Municipal Cathedral.
* The Fountains Park.
* Portales Palace.
* Santa Teresa Convent.
* The impressive Archaeological Museum.
* The large Cristo statue.
* The High Valley.
* Trek to the Tunari mountain range.
* Pairumani Park (includes an easy daily trip).
* Jurassic Park - the amazing Torotoro National Park.
