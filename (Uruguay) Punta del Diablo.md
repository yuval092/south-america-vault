# Punta del Diablo

Fishing village located in southeast of country touching border with neighboring Brazil and known as attractive surf destination attracting many travelers.

**Activities:**
* The village is serene and calm but you can enjoy walking trips on Playa de la Viuda beach
* Camping and tent areas
* Fishing areas
* Wave surfing, kitesurfing
* Horse riding
* Tour to Santa Teresa Fortress
* Day trip to Black Lagoon
* Tour in Santa Teresa National Park
* Renting beach cabins
* Sandboarding in dunes
* Walking on beach and reaching viewpoint overlooking village
* Trip in Fishermen's Beach

**Tip:**
Bring mosquito repellent.
