# Puerto Viejo

Border town in the south along the Caribbean coast, used for crossing to Panama. A must-visit. Recommended:
* Black Beach (Playa Negra) and spectacular Punta Uva Beach.
* Puerto Viejo Beach walk.
* Surf camps.
* Canopy zip-lines.
* Artisan market.
* Moped rental.
* Playa Cocles surf beach.
* Playa Blanca nature reserve beach.
* Sunrise watching.
* Bracelet stalls.
* Reggae parties.
* SUP lessons.
* Cacao plantation tour.
* Yoga classes.
* Indigenous nature reserve.
* Trip to Playa Chiquita.
* National Wildlife Refuge.
* Jaguar/Sloth Rescue Center.
* Snorkeling.
* Waterfall rappelling.
