# Galápagos Islands

Wonderful natural reserve located ~1000km west of Ecuador. Uncompromising destination for many travelers. Attractions:
* El Chato Giant Tortoise Reserve.
* Sailing to Tortuga Bay.
* Lava tunnels.
* Diving in breathtaking sites.
* Meeting sharks.
* Arrival: Flight from Quito/Guayaquil to San Cristobal or Santa Cruz.
* Fees: $20 flight tax + $100 entry tax.
* Seasons: Nov-May (hot, little rain); Jun-Oct (wet but cheaper).
