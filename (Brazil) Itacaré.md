# Itacaré

Small popular village located between Porto Seguro and Salvador/Morro. Arrival via shuttle from Morro, boat to Valença then bus, direct bus from Porto Seguro, or ferry from Salvador to Bom Despacho then bus. Also accessible by flight to Ilhéus. Features:
* Golden beaches, white sand, tropical vegetation (movie-like appearance).
* Famous beach: The Hidden Beach (Resende/Tiririca area) reached by foot.
* Engenhoca Beach (jungle walk, palm trees, black sand).
* Severe Warning: In 2023 season, reports of strong drift and real drowning danger.
