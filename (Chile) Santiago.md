# Santiago

The capital city, modern and large with rich nightlife in Bellavista and Santa Lucia neighborhoods. Recommended:
* City center, San Francisco Church, Plaza de la Constitución.
* Cerro Santa Lucia viewpoint (fortress remains, manicured green area).
* Metropolitan Park.
* Sky Costanera / Gran Torre (tallest building in continent).
* Fantasilandia amusement park.
* Cajón del Maipo lagoon in snowy mountains (1 hr away).
* Access to Viña del Mar and Valparaíso.
* Winery tours, skiing in winter.
* Safari (open walking and jeep tours - check availability).
* San Alfonso del Mar resort (world's largest pool) in Algarrobo (100km away).
* Sewell ghost town (50km away).
