# La Macarena

A rare river colored in 5 colors - yellow, green, blue, black, and red. The colors appear between June and November. The river is located in the Sierra de la Macarena National Park, near the town of La Macarena. Attractions include:
* The famous Caño Cristales tour.
* Tour to Caño Cristalitos (animals and viewpoint).
* Caño Piedra stream (natural pools).
* Raudal tour (ancient city visit).
* Walking along the river independently.
* Horseback riding and bike rental.
* Arrival: Flight from Medellín, Cali, Bogotá, or Villavicencio (often cheaper). Land travel from Villavicencio is ~8 hours. From Cali, reach Neiva then 10 hours by land.
