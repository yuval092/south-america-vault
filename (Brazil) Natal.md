# Natal

Transit city to Pipa. Arrival by flight/bus. Features:
* Buggy tours in the famous Dunes Park.
* Sandboarding.
* World's largest Cashew Tree at Pirangi do Norte.
* Forte dos Reis Magos (star-shaped fortress).
* Beautiful beaches.
* Newton Navarro Bridge (1.8 km long, spectacular).
* Barreira do Inferno rocket launch site (free entry).
* Midway Mall with huge supermarket.
