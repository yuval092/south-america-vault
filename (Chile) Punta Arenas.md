# Punta Arenas

Transit town from Ushuaia to Puerto Natales. Here you will find:
* Duty-free zone (Zona Franca) for shopping, mainly camping gear.
* Main attraction: Penguin colony viewing.
* Magallanes National Reserve offering forest with snowy mountains.
* Cross of the Seas Trek (Cruz de los Mares): Difficult 2-3 day trek leading to the meeting point of the Pacific and Atlantic oceans (marked by giant metal cross).
* Dientes de Navarino Trek: Considered one of the most beautiful, with a mountain range looking like teeth; duration 4-6 days.
