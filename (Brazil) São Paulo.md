# São Paulo

The largest city in Brazil, located between Rio and Florianópolis. You will find:
* Markets, huge malls, cathedrals, churches, and giant skyscrapers.
* Paulista Avenue with clothing stores, bars, and cafes (street performances, food stalls, and art on Sundays).
* 'Temple of Solomon': A precise replica of the Temple including the Altar and Holy of Holies - a must-see.
* Pelé Museum in nearby Santos.
* Japanese Quarter (Liberdade) offering a glimpse into the Japanese community with a temple and restaurants.
* Huge Ibirapuera Park.
* Rich nightlife; hipster Augusta neighborhood with restaurants and bars.
* City view from the 46th floor of Edifício Itália.
* Huge Jewish community with kosher supermarkets and restaurants.
* Recommended areas to stay: Vila Madalena or near Paulista Avenue.
