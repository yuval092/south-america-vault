# Jericoacoara

Brazil's paradise featuring amazing beaches, giant dunes, and breathtaking lagoons. A must-visit. Highlights:
* One of the world's most important surf spots.
* Jeep/buggy tours, surfing, kitesurfing, capoeira, horseback riding, fishing, seahorse finding, lagoon slide.
* East Coast: Slanted tree, giant rock in water, famous hammock.
* Paradise Lagoon with hammocks.
* Arrival: Direct flight, or bus/jeep from Fortaleza, or jeep from Lençóis Maranhenses (via São Luís/Barreirinhas).
* Preá town (12km away) recommended for kitesurfers seeking calm.
* Entrance tax: 30 Reais.
