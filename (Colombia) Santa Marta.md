# Santa Marta

Northern city on the Caribbean coast characterized by warm weather and vacation atmosphere. From here you can reach nearby Taganga. Enjoy:
* Impressive marina.
* Tayrona Gold Museum.
* Aquarium tour.
* Lovers Park (Parque de los Novios).
* El Dorado bird park.
* Zazu Plaza shopping center.
* Electronics market.
* Tour of Marinka waterfalls.
* Valencia waterfalls and natural pool.
* Visit Seven Waves Beach, Crystal Beach, Grande Beach.
* Lost City Trek.
* Tayrona Park visit.
* Diving and snorkeling schools.
* Horseback riding.
* Note: For travel to Cartagena, choose bus lines (Berlinas/Marsol) passing on the day route due to robbery risk. Shuttles to Tayrona available.
