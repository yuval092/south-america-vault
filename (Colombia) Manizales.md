# Manizales

A city in the northern coffee region about two hours from Salento, full of students and diverse nightlife. Possesses spectacular views and a spring-like and rainy climate, treks, and attractions. In the city and its surroundings you can visit:
* Plaza Bolívar.
* Aerial cable car crossing the city.
* Chipre observatory with amazing sunsets.
* El Cable nightlife area.
* Tango and Bachata dance course.
* Shopping at the large Mallplaza.
* Recinto del Pensamiento bird park.
* Coffee farms in the village of Chinchiná.
* About two hours from Manizales is Parque del Café - the largest amusement park in Colombia.
