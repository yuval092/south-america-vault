# Manuel Antonio National Park

Breathtaking paradise town in the west known for the National Park and spectacular beaches. In the park area and surroundings:
* Jungle hiking trails.
* Waterfall rappelling.
* Dominical waterfalls.
* Beach walks.
* Kayaking.
* Surf lessons/rentals.
* Diving and snorkeling.
* Canoe sailing.
* Hot springs.
* Nahomi Park.
* Marina walk.
* Trip to Espadilla Beach.
* Fishing.
* Bird watching.
* Guided park tours.
* Catamaran sailing.
* Note: Beware of attempts to scam tourists at park entrance.
