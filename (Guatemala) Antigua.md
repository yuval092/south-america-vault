# Antigua

A colonial city located south of the City and considered a must-visit destination for many travelers due to the large amount of attractions.

**In and around the city you can visit:**
* Pacaya Volcano National Park - a half-day trek where you can roast marshmallows on the hot stones
* Acatenango Volcano trek and watching lava eruptions from Fuego Volcano at night (difficult trek, about 5 hours climb on a steep slope)
* Another observation point for Fuego Volcano
* Climb to the Cross Viewpoint (Cerro de la Cruz) - a walking trail of about 20 minutes
* Trek to Volcán de Agua
* Spanish lessons
* Yoga classes
* Daily tour to Lake Atitlán
* Off-road karting tours
* Daily tour of Antigua
* Trip to Semuc Champey
* Market tours in the city
* Bicycle tours
* Chocolate museum and workshops
* Hobbitenango viewpoint - structures inspired by The Hobbit and Lord of the Rings (arrival by Uber, the place has attractions such as mini golf, archery, giant swing, and more), includes a walking trail and attractions complex, about 20 minutes drive by Uber
* Visit to El Pilar Nature Reserve which includes forests, natural pools, and walking trails
