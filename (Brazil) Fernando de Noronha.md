# Fernando de Noronha

Island northeast of Natal, reachable only by flight (approx 1 hour) from Recife or Natal. Brazil's pearl - where Galapagos meets San Andres and Easter Island. Highlights:
* Nature reserve with reefs and animals.
* Rare beaches.
* Swim/dive with dolphins, sharks, green turtles, and giant rays.
