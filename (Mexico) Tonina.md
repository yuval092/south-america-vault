# Tonina

About three hours drive from San Cristobal is the tallest Mayan pyramid in the world and the tallest pyramid in Latin America - Tonina. Arrival to Tonina is from the town of Ocosingo, which is reached from San Cristobal. It can be visited as a day trip from San Cristobal.
