# El Paredón

A surfing town located in the west of the country along the Pacific Ocean.

**Activities:**
* In this amazing town you can mainly enjoy relaxation
* Surf camp and equipment rental
* Snorkeling
* Beach volleyball
* Kayaking in the Acome River and reaching the Sipacate-Naranjo National Park
* Yoga classes
* Trip to the pastoral Playa Barra beach
* Watching beautiful sunsets/sunrises
