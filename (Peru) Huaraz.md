# Huaraz

Mountainous city located at 3000 meters height and known as the Trekking Capital of South America offering plenty of extreme attractions and amazing treks. Arrival to city by bus from Lima or Trujillo.

**City Sights:**
* Main Square Plaza de Armas
* Willcain National Monument
* Pastoruri Cemetery
* Soledad Church
* San Francisco Church Park
* Archaeological Museum
* Hot springs
* The Cross overlooking the city
* Local market

**Treks & Activities:**
* Huayhuash Trek
* Santa Cruz Trek
* Shinodae Trek
* Alpamayo Trek
* Peak climbing on Cordillera Blanca
* Huascarán peak at 6768 meters height
* Pastoruri Glacier
* Laguna 69
* Laguna Paron
* Laguna Churup
* Laguna Llanganuco
* Laguna 513
* Trip to Chavín town, Chavín de Huántar temple (click for detailed details)
* Waterfall rappelling
* Glacier climbing
* Rock climbing
* Wall climbing and courses
* Rafting
* Horse tours
* Zipline
* Paragliding and Bungee
