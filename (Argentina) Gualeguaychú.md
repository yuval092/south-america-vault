# Gualeguaychú

The carnival of Argentina - a town famous for the carnival that takes place in the city every Saturday night and holidays in January and February and is considered the third largest carnival in the world and the second on the continent after Rio.

**Logistics:**
* Located about 3 hours drive from Buenos Aires (direct bus from Retiro terminal arriving at Gualeguaychú terminal, round trip about $20).
* Especially impressive costume parades and a different experience from the one in Brazil.
* Parade hours from 22:00 to 02:00.

**Activities & Cost:**
* During the day there are beach parties on the river bank, visit Solar del Este beach where you will find young people and a worth-it party costing about $10.
* The parades cost about $12, it is advisable to book accommodation in advance due to the load during the carnival.
