# San Gil

One of the most beautiful towns in Colombia, located in the east of the country and known as a town of non-stop attractions. In this stunning town, you can reach:
* The city cathedral Santa Cruz.
* Liberty Park (Parque La Libertad).
* San Gil Central Park.
* San Gil Market.
* Trip to the Love Locks Bridge and river viewpoint.
* Tour of the Pozo Azul nature reserve.
* Impressive cave tour.
* Rafting and professional kayaking.
* Trip to the nearby Juan Curi waterfalls.
* Tour of the town of Barichara and view of the Chicamocha Canyon.
* Paragliding in Chicamocha Canyon.
* Bungee jumping.
* Bicycle tours.
* Many camping areas.
