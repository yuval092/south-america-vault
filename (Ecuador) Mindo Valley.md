# Mindo Valley

Small village in the north not far from Quito. From the small village you can go to:
* Large Butterfly Park.
* Village Fish Aquarium.
* Frog Garden and vocal concert.
* Nambillo Waterfalls.
* La Reina Waterfall trip.
* Bird sanctuary.
* Las Tangaras Reserve (huge variety of birds).
* Minjoy Park cable car.
* Vehicular visit to Tartiva Park.
* Tour of green Rio Bravo Reserve.
* Bamboo Labyrinth.
* El Pahuma Orchid Reserve and Cloud Forest Reserve.
