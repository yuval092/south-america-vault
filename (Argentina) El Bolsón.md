# El Bolsón

A pastoral town in a green and magical area. Arrival by bus from Bariloche.

**In the El Bolsón area you can do:**
* The Rio Azul treks
* The Indian Head trek (mountain looking like an Indian head)
* Trek to the secret waterfall Cerro Piltriquitrón
* Los Alerces National Park
* Cajón de Azul National Park including accommodation in cabins (click for post | click for post 2)
* Trek to the Giant Rocks Canyon and Sculpture Forest
* The magical forest Belenus including sculptures of fairies, wizards, dragons, unicorn and more

**Info:**
El Bolsón market on Tuesdays, Thursdays and Saturdays.
