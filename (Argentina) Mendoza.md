# Mendoza

The wine home of Argentina, offering tours of fine vineyards and wineries.

**Arrival:**
Reached from Córdoba, Salta, Bariloche and Santiago. Located near Mount Aconcagua which is one of the highest in the world and the highest on the continent.

**Activities:**
* Winery tour in nearby Maipú by bicycle or on foot with wine tasting
* Treks to climb peaks in the nearby Andes (click for Vallecitos trek post)
* Horse riding
* Rafting
* The famous Inca Bridge (Puente del Inca) built entirely of stone (about 4 hours from Mendoza near the border with Chile - click for Google location and photos)
* Watching condors in Aconcagua Park
* Visit to Termas Cacheuta hot springs
* Visit to San Juan town and from there to Valle de la Luna Park which resembles the Grand Canyon or the moon with giant boulders
