# Rio de Janeiro

The city is famous for its Carnival, football, famous beaches, and favelas. In the city, you will find:
* The Maracanã football stadium.
* The crazy SAARA market on Rua da Alfandega.
* The famous Uruguaiana market where you can find Havaianas flip-flops.
* The Hippie Market offering art and fashion accessories every Sunday.
* Favela tours (slums and shantytowns).
* Arpoador Beach between Ipanema and Copacabana (short hike to a giant rock).
* Leblon Beach in Ipanema and watching the sunset on the small mountain.
* Joatinga Beach with transparent water.
* The Red Beach (Praia Vermelha).
* Free gym on the beach between Copa and Ipanema.
* Pedra do Telégrafo rock (allows for photos looking like you're hanging in mid-air; location: Barra da Guaratiba).
* Corcovado Mountain with the famous Christ the Redeemer statue (one of the New Seven Wonders of the World).
* Sugarloaf Mountain viewpoint with optional climbing attraction.
* Leme Hill with a short trail.
* Tijuca National Park in the heart of the city (free entry) offering treks (jungle, caves, bridges, waterfalls, rivers).
* Pedra Bonita in Tijuca Park (climbable via a daily trek).
* Two Brothers Mountain (Dois Irmãos) offering an easy daily trek.
* Cinelândia Square and the Municipal Cathedral.
* The world's largest graffiti mural on Olympic Boulevard.
* The famous Selarón Steps in Santa Teresa.
* Sambadrome (official Carnival venue).
* Helicopter tour over major sites.
* Hang gliding over the city landscapes.
* Rio Star, the largest Ferris wheel in Latin America.
* Rodrigo de Freitas Lagoon.
* Botanical Gardens on Rua Jardim Botânico.
* Rio Zoo.
* Basilica Santuario (church reached by cable car).
* Museum of Tomorrow offering a look at the future.
* Walking and biking tours of the city.
