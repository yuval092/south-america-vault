# David

The second largest city in Panama.

**From here you can:**
* Cross the border to Costa Rica
* Visit Boquete and Volcán Barú
* Watch Humpback whales in Chiriquí Gulf (between 15.7 to 15.10)
* Visit Gulf of Chiriquí National Park which is a marine national park with over 20 islands and coral reefs
* Access Isla Boca Brava which is an especially green island with monkeys, turtles, diving around the island

**In the city:**
* Visit Catedral de San José de David
* Playa La Barqueta beach

**Warning:**
Avoid night travel from David to Bocas del Toro, the road is dark, chance of fog and dangerous.
