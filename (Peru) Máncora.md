# Máncora

Coastal town with warm and desert weather in north of country known for its surfing and relaxation areas.

**Attractions:**
* Markets, restaurants, parties, bars, hostels, hotels
* Horse riding on beach
* Lighthouse viewpoint
* ATVs
* Jet skis
* Kitesurfing
* Wave surfing
* Banana boat sailing
* Swimming with sea turtles and diving
