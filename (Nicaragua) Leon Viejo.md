# Leon Viejo

The old city of Leon, which was completely destroyed in 1609 by the eruption of Momotombo volcano. The old city was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. You can tour among the ruins buried under the lava and also see reconstructions of central buildings. The site has an excellent viewpoint of the volcano and Lake Nicaragua.
