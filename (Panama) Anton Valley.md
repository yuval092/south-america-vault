# Anton Valley

A picturesque town about two hours bus drive from Panama City, sitting in a huge volcanic crater, the second largest in the country.

**Surroundings & Activities:**
* Surrounded by green area, waterfalls, natural pools and hikes
* Canopy attraction
* 4-hour trek to El Palmar waterfalls
* Trip to El Chorro Macho waterfall passing through the jungle with bridges and streams
* Butterfly Heaven park (click for details)
* Visit to El Nispero Zoo and Botanical Garden
* Dipping in hot springs
* Visit to the illustrated rocks in the Archaeological Park - giant rocks called La Piedra Pintada with illustrations from the pre-Columbian era
