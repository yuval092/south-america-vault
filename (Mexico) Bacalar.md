# Bacalar

Bacalar Lagoon is one of the most beautiful places on the eastern side of Mexico and is considered a must-see. You can do a whole range of activities such as:
* Trip to the Lagoon of Seven Colors.
* Day trip to the natural water pool Cenote Azul.
* Kayaking in Bacalar Lagoon.
* Tour of Fort San Felipe.
* Long swings along the lagoon.
* Horseback riding tours.
* Kayaking in Cenote de la Bruja reserve.
* Short distance from the city is a Mennonite village (Amish community | Ejido Salamanca) where time stood still and the village operates like 500 years ago.
