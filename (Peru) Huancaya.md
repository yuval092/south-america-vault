# Huancaya

A small town close to Lima (arrival by bus), a place with amazing nature and amazing views - mountains, trees, streams in spectacular colors, waterfalls, lagoons - basically, lots of beautiful blue and green.

**Arrival:**
Take a bus to Huancayo and from there a taxi to Huancaya. You can see more arrival ways - click for post with details and photos.
