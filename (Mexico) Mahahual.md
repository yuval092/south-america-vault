# Mahahual

Mahahual beach town is close to the border with Belize, about half an hour from Bacalar. It offers:
* Spectacular beaches with hammocks in the water.
* Clear water and lots of calm and quiet.
* Snorkeling and diving.
* Kitesurfing or just lounging on the beach.
