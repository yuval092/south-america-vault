# Montevideo

Capital of Uruguay and serves as an important port city and largest in country considered a must-destination when arriving in Uruguay.

**Activities:**
* Trip in city center - Old City and touristy pedestrian street
* Lively Independence Square
* La Rambla famous promenade on beach side
* 18 de Julio Avenue packed with shops and Artists Fair
* Carnival Museum
* Villa Dolores Zoo
* Trip in Rodó Park and sailing in lake
* Bouza Bodega Boutique wine factory and tasting
* Football Museum
* Nightlife area in Mitre, Bacacay and Sarandí streets
* Horse racing
* Sailing to Isla de Flores historic island and watching sea lions
* Walking on Pocitos Beach promenade
* Lecocq Animal Park
* Punta Carretas large shopping center
