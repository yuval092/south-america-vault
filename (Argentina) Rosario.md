# Rosario

One of the most vibrant cities in Argentina, some say the most beautiful women in the country live here.

**The main attraction is a tour of:**
* Leo Messi's life points and graffiti sites
* One of the cheapest skydiving in the continent
* The largest casino in South America
* Visit to Che Guevara sites
* The giant Paseo del Caminante viewpoint
* Sailing on the Parana River
* Football games
* Karting
* Weekend markets
* The observatory in Urquiza Park and rich nightlife
