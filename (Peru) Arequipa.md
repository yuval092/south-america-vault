# Arequipa

The second largest city in the country and also known as 'The White City'. The high and beautiful city offers a variety of action and activity.

**Highlights:**
* Main Square Plaza de Armas
* Plaza San Francisco
* Jesus Church
* The huge Santa Catalina Monastery close to 20,000 square meters
* Sachaca city viewpoint
* Goyeneche Palace
* Visit to Salinas and Aguada Blanca National Reserve and huge Laguna de Salinas
* Tour of Salinas de Huito salt deserts resembling Salar de Uyuni in Bolivia where you can take impressive photos in vast white spaces - the nature reserve and salt deserts reach 3500 meters height
* Visit to Ruta del Sillar quarries where you will see especially impressive sculptures and carvings (click to view photos)
* Visit to the popular Colca Canyon where you can watch giant birds - the famous Condors, do treks in the spectacular canyon and even sleep in one of the villages
