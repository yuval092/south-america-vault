# Utila

A paradise island for divers known as an impressive diving site thanks to its location in the Caribbean Sea and clear water visibility, situated opposite the shores of La Ceiba from where you can reach the island by ferry taking about 45 minutes.

**In the magical and beautiful island you can:**
* Do diving courses of all kinds
* Apprenticeship in diving schools
* Diving with whale sharks
* Snorkeling
* Enjoy the authentic nightlife and atmosphere in the place
* Chill at the quiet Bando Beach
* Diving to the coral reef
* Diving at the popular Black Hills site
* Daily tour to the Iguana Center
