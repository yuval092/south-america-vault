# Ibitipoca State Park

Ibitipoca nature reserve is located about 4 hours from Rio on the way to Ouro Preto. It offers:
* Forest with spectacular waterfalls and caves.
* Main attraction: A waterfall allowing you to sit at the edge of the cliff (note: this is dangerous, exercise caution).
