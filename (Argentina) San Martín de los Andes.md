# San Martín de los Andes

A ski town located between Bariloche and Pucón, sitting between mountains and a spectacular lake.

**Highlights:**
* San Martín serves as a departure point for the famous Seven Lakes route and Lanín National Park where you can climb Lanín Volcano and visit famous lakes, rivers and fishing activities.
* The ski resort in town is considered the best in Argentina and one of the best on the continent.
* You can find campsites on the lake shore in the town's vicinity.
* You can visit the Arrayan viewpoint and Bandurrias.
* Visit the village Villa La Angostura located at the end of the Seven Lakes. The village is built entirely of wooden houses and from there you reach the Arrayanes Forest also called Bambi Forest and strongly resembles a forest from fairytales.
* Quila Quina Reserve where the Mapuche tribe members reside.
