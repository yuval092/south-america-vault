# Sonsonate

A route of 32 km located between the cities Ahuachapán and Sonsonate along spectacular blooming decorating house gardens in the towns on the route. It is recommended to arrive between November and February for the period when the blooming is at its peak.

**Along the route you can:**
* Swim in Los Chorros de la Calera waterfalls
* Trek to the Seven Waterfalls in Juayúa
* Move between destinations on local buses
* Walk along the route
* Ride bicycles or horses

**Route Info:**
* The route can be done in both directions
* It passes through Concepción de Ataco - Apaneca - Salcoatitán - Juayúa - Nahuizalco
