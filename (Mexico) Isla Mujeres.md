# Isla Mujeres

A magical Caribbean island located east of Cancun offering some of the clearest and most beautiful beaches in Mexico. Access to the island is via a short ferry from Cancun's port (Puerto Juarez) or the Hotel Zone port (Playa Caracol). Points of interest include:
* Visit the North Beach (Playa Norte).
* The quiet Garrafon Reef Park.
* Diving and snorkeling sites.
* Swimming with whale sharks and dolphins.
* Day cruise to Isla Contoy.
* Bicycle rentals and tours.
* Golf cart rentals.
* Visit the turtle farm (Tortugranja).
* Jewelry stalls.
* Walking on the Punta Sur promenade and the beautiful viewpoint.

* Diving to the underwater sculpture museum - a spectacular experience for divers.
* Aquatic Funday Water Park offering all facilities with unlimited food and drink.
