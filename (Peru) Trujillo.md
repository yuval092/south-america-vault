# Trujillo

The third largest city in Peru after Lima and Arequipa located on Peruvian coastline and characterized by its ancient history.

**Attractions:**
* Temple of Sun and Moon
* Rainbow Temple-Dragon Temple
* El Brujo Archaeological Center
* Santa Clara Temple
* Chan Chan city
* Sandboarding
* Iturregui Palace

**Note:**
City serves as transit point to Chicama town where longest wave in world is found. Can visit nearby fishing village Huanchaco. Click here for detailed info on the city.
