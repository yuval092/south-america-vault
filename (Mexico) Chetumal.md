# Chetumal

The border town of Chetumal serves as a crossing point for land crossing to Belize City or direct boat to the islands of Belize. From the town, you can:
* Reach Bacalar Lagoon.
* Visit the town's promenade.
* Visit the Mayan Culture Museum.
* Trip to the Zoo.
* Visit the ruins and pyramid of Dzibanche.
