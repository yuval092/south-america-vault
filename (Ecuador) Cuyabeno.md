# Cuyabeno

Second largest nature reserve in the country, located in the east, part of the Amazon forest. Infinite dense jungle and wild rivers. Animals everywhere: crocodiles, monkeys, anacondas, dolphins, etc.
* Tours of 2-3 nights+ in cabins (no tents).
* Includes river sailing and indigenous community visit.
* Popular because it is easier to reach and cheaper than Yasuni.
