# Colca Canyon

An especially impressive and deep canyon and one of the most known attractions in Peru - watching Condors.

**Activities:**
* Impressive Condor Cross viewpoint
* Zipline in the canyon
* Colca Canyon trek which is a two-day trek combining hot springs, villages and true Peruvian experience
* You can sleep in hostels in Yahuar village and along the river
