# Copán Ruinas

An especially impressive Maya antiquities site located in the west of the country and a stopover point between La Ceiba in Honduras and Antigua in Guatemala.

**At the site you will find:**
* Pyramids
* Ancient structures with trees and roots bursting from them
* Giant statues
* Large wall decorations
* Tour of Rosalila Temple pyramid
* Visit to the Bird Park at Macaw Mountain
* Trip to Cisne Farm and horse riding
* Departure for hot springs near the site
* Viewing the ancient amphitheater
* Visit to the Archaeological Museum
