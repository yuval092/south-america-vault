# Vilcabamba

Small lovely town in the south bordering Peru. Activities:
* Tour of Valley of Longevity.
* Many horse farms.
* Cerro Mandango trail and village viewpoint.
* El Palto waterfall tour.
* Lotus Health Farm.
* Spas.
* Horseback riding.
* Stream tours and short treks.
