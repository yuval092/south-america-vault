# Holbox

A quiet and calm island north of Cancun and opposite the shores of the town of Chiquila (accessible by bus from Cancun, then ferry). On the peaceful island, you can enjoy:
* Watching bioluminescent plankton glowing in the dark.
* Diving and snorkeling.
* Diving with whale sharks.
* Visit Punta Mosquito beach, considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the world.
* Visit Punta Coco beach.
* Flamingo tour.
* Trip to Punta Mosquito area.
* Golf cart rental and island tour.
* Yoga and meditation classes.
* Island tour.
* Relaxing in hammocks on the beach.
* Day trip to Yalahau and swimming in the lagoon.
