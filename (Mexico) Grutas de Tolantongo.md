# Grutas de Tolantongo

Stunning attraction located 3 hours north of Mexico City near the town of Hidalgo. In the place, you will find stunningly beautiful natural pools. You will find waterfalls, tunnels, rivers, bridges, and treks. Arrival by car (Waze: Grutas de Tolantongo), taxi (which will wait for you and bring you back), or bus - take bus from Mexico City Terminal Norte to Ixmiquilpan and from there walk or taxi to Terminal San Antonio where you will find buses to the pools. By bus, you will have to sleep a night there.
