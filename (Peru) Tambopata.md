# Tambopata

Nature reserve where you can watch largest Macaw parrot flocks in the world.

**Activities:**
* Diverse activities including sailing and jungle tours
* Animal watching
* Rich vegetation
* Sailing to Sandoval Lake
* Sailing to Tambopata River
* Ecological Park tour
* Billinghurst Bridge crossing
* Bird tour
* Meeting local villages
* Endangered animal shelter
* Monkey Island (click for post with photos and details)
* Butterfly area
* Fishing experience
* Kayaking in Amazon and ziplines

**Info:**
Suitable for those looking for cheap tour and suitable for up to 3-4 visit days, survival cannot be done, accommodation in most cases outside reserve. Arrival to reserve from Puerto Maldonado.
