# Cuenca

Third largest city, located in the south. Modern city with accommodation, restaurants, bars, markets. Sites:
* El Cajas National Park (30 min away; dozens of trails, lagoons, unique vegetation, alpacas; altitude 4000m).
* Main Cathedral (Inmaculada) - one of the largest in the continent.
* Old City visit.
* Del Rio Shopping Center.
* Hot springs.
* Turi Viewpoint.
* Paradise Park.
* City Zoo.
* Sombrero Museum.
* Pumapungo Museum (contemporary art).
* October 9 Market (food).
* Centro Compras Market (clothes/art).
* Flower Market.
* Ingapirca Archaeological Site visit (1.5 hours north).
