# Cusco

The capital city of the Inca tribe, special and colorful, towering to a height of 3400 meters and serving as a must-destination for Israelis.

**Arrival:**
Can be reached by bus from Puno, Arequipa and Lima (from the latter advisable to take a flight due to route length, dangerous curves and night travel - avoid bus travel on Lima-Cusco line). Can arrive from Cusco to Pisac with minibus (Google Maps location Collectivo to Pisac).

**Popular Treks:**
* **Inca Trail:** The only official trek leading directly to Machu Picchu site, 2-4 days long.
* **Salkantay Trek:** Includes Humantay Lagoon, Salkantay summit and walking along river leading to train tracks and Machu Picchu site.
* **Ausangate Trek:** Spectacular trek to Ausangate mountain peaks. Can be done by foot or on horses, the trek is not easy.

**Note:** For some treks there is an option to arrive or return by train and alternatively return by car - always prefer return by train because it is significantly safer than car. For additional treks and details on Inca Trail, Salkantay, Choquequirao and Lares - click here for detailed routes.
