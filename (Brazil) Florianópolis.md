# Florianópolis

One of the most popular and safest tourist destinations in Brazil - sun, sea, beaches, parties, lagoons, and dunes. Locals call it Floripa. Highlights:
* Paradise for beach lovers and surfers (waves considered some of the best in the world).
* Famous nightlife, popular P12 club, and pool parties.
* Popular beaches: Barra da Lagoa, Lagoa da Conceição, Mole, and Joaquina.
* Old Town, November 15 Square, and churches.
* Alligator Alley river (alligators found next to a huge shopping center).
* Sailing to Campeche Island.
* Visit to fortresses.
* São Joaquim National Park (impressive ridges, streams, waterfalls, wild nature - car recommended).
* Jurerê neighborhood (fake market found in nearby Canasvieiras).
* Sailing from Jurerê to the small island Ilha do Francês opposite.
