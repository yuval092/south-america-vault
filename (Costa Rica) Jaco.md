# Jaco

Popular surf town located in the west along the Pacific coast. In this hot town you can do:
* Surfing, kitesurfing, surf schools/rentals.
* Walk on touristy Jaco Beach.
* Pura Vida green nature reserve.
* Waterfalls tour.
* Bijagual Road (waterfall and pools).
* Snake farm.
* Waterfall climbing.
* Zip-lines.
* Mirador de Jaco viewpoint.
* Butterfly farm.
* Cable car.
* ATV rental.
* Horseback riding.
* National Park tour.
* Crocodile tour.
* Playa Linda and Playa Hermosa.
* Boat to Santa Teresa available with Zuma Tours.
