# Montañita

Colorful and bustling surf town with vacation atmosphere; a must-visit. 3.5 hours bus from Guayaquil. Enjoy:
* Nightlife: Parties, restaurants, festivals.
* Markets, shopping stalls.
* Surf schools/rentals.
* Kitesurfing.
* Montañita Beach and nearby Olon Beach.
* Diving and snorkeling.
* Paragliding.
* Horseback riding.
* Machalilla National Park tour.
* Dos Mangas waterfalls tour.
* Sailing to Isla de la Plata ('Poor Man's Galapagos').
* White Grave.
* Starfish viewpoint on beach.
* Whale watching.
