# Roatán

A spectacular Caribbean island which is known as one of the three Bay Islands of Honduras containing magical beaches and sports activities and serves as one of the most beautiful diving sites on the continent.

**In the beautiful island you can do a large variety of attractions:**
* Visit the most popular West Bay beach
* Horse riding trips
* Tour of Gumbalimba Park where you can watch many animals and special vegetation
* Arrival at Maya Key beach where you can snorkel and reach an animal shelter
* Diving courses of all kinds
* Visit to Manawakie Park including sloths and monkeys
* Little French Key extreme beach complex where it is recommended to kayak
* Local restaurants alongside the beach
* Swimming with dolphins
* Snorkeling and enjoying chill in hammocks and swings
* You can visit the Blue Channel diving site
* Tour of the Iguana Park
* Tour of the Butterfly Garden
