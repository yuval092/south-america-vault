# Teotihuacan

Must-visit at the breathtaking pyramids site and tour of the Pyramid of the Sun - the largest pyramid in Latin America and second largest in the world. You can view the Pyramid of the Sun from a hot air balloon. Accessible by car or taxi (about an hour drive) or bus/metro (about two hours drive). Entrance fee about $5.
