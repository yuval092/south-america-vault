# Las Pozas De Xilitla

Located 7 hours north of Mexico City is a huge forest with waterfalls, natural pools, and spectacular sculptures intended to simulate a magical paradise, and the result is impressive. The pools are located at an altitude of 2000m (Las Pozas De Xilitla).
