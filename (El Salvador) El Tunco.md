# El Tunco

An authentic surfers village living from foreign and domestic tourism, a safe area and one of the central destinations when visiting El Salvador.

**Surfing:**
* Professional surfers claim this is one of the best surfing destinations in Central America thanks to high-quality spots
* El Sunzal is the central and most popular spot in El Salvador and one of the most famous in the world
* Additional spots are Bocana, Bocanita, and Punta Roca

**Atmosphere & Activities:**
* The village is very small with an atmosphere of relaxation and surfing
* You will find in the village surf clubs, restaurants, shakes, yoga, massages, nightlife and many tourists
* For those looking for nightlife - arrive on the weekend
* You can visit Tamanique waterfalls about 45 min from El Tunco
