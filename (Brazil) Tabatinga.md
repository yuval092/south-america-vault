# Tabatinga

Border town deep in the Amazon, reached by boat from Manaus. From here continue to Iquitos (Peru) or Leticia (Colombia). Boat details:
* Fast boat: ~36 hours, ~$150 (varies by demand).
* Slow boat: Half price, takes 3.5 days.
