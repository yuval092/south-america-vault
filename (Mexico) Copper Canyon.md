# Copper Canyon

Located in the Sierra Madre reserve desert and composed of twenty canyons estimated to be 7 times larger than the Grand Canyon. You can hike, trek, ride horses, bike, or take a tour on the El Chepe train which is the highlight of the visit. You can reach the train from the city of Chihuahua or Los Mochis (located 5 hours north of Mazatlan). The train leaves from one city and ends in the other - route length 650 km, 39 bridges, 86 tunnels. Route duration 13 hours and you can stop to sleep in the canyon.
