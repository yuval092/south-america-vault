# Oaxaca

An authentic and colorful city in the south of the country characterized by an abundance of art products and handicrafts. In the colorful city, you can tour:
* The city square and textile stalls.
* November 20 Food Market.
* Benito Juarez Downtown Market.
* Visit the Animal Rescue and Jaguar Conservation Center.
* Mitla archaeological site.
* Monte Alban ruins.
* Day trip to Hierve el Agua nature reserve and mineral pools located on a mountain cliff with insane views - a must-see.
* The Tule Tree (Arbol de Tule) which is the widest tree in the world.
* Pueblos Mancomunados village tour including hanging bridges, zip-lines, and impressive views.
* Departure for treks near the village of San Jose del Pacifico.
