# Panajachel

The largest town on Lake Atitlán sitting in the northeastern part of the lake.

**Activities:**
* In the calm town you can take a cruise on the beautiful lake
* Visit the Iximche archaeological site
* Motorcycle trip around the lake
* Trip in the Atitlán Nature Reserve
* Tour of the town's colorful market
* Bicycle rental and walking trails
* Visit the Chocolate Museum
* Volcano tour
* Kayaking in the reserve
* Ziplines
* Tour of the Butterfly Park and Monkey Park
