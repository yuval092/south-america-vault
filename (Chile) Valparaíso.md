# Valparaíso

Colorful city on Chile's coast, 1.5 hours from Santiago, second largest in country. Features:
* Boasts murals and graffiti.
* Main attraction is simply walking around and being impressed.
* Can continue to Algarrobo (San Alfonso resort) 1 hour away.
