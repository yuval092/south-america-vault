# Minca

A village in the mountains, about an hour's drive from Taganga or Santa Marta. Some call it the pearl of northern Colombia due to the spectacular mountain scenery and green nature.
* Popular attraction: The largest hammock in the world overlooking the beautiful view.
* Daily trek to various waterfalls around Minca.
* Accessible by shuttles or taxi.
