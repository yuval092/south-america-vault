# Valladolid

The town of Valladolid located in the southeast of the country in the Yucatan region, accessible by direct bus from Cancun. From here you can go to:
* The famous Chichen Itza ruins, recognized as one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. At the ancient archaeological site, you can see the Great Pyramid, guided tours, ancient structures, temples, refreshments, water wells, and souvenir stalls.
* In the town, you can visit the central square Parque Principal Francisco.
* Visit Calzada de los Frailes, a beautiful street leading to the convent.
* Cenote Zaci, the huge Cenote Suytun, Cenote Oxman with a Tarzan rope, and other cenotes within driving distance.
* Visit the Ek Balam archaeological site of the Maya people.
* Arrival by ADO bus.
