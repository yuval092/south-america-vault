# Sambo Creek

Sambo Creek is a small village 25 minutes from La Ceiba and from it you can depart on a daily cruise or with accommodation in Cayos Cochinos. The cruise length is about 45 minutes.

**Highlights:**
* The islands are small and beautiful with white beaches, coral reefs and the local Garifuna tribe.
