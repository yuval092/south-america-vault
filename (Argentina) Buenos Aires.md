# Buenos Aires

The capital city and a must-visit destination in Argentina. One of the largest and most interesting cities on the continent, offering rich nightlife, markets, shopping centers, and attractions.

**You can walk around:**
* The city center
* Plaza de Mayo which is the central square and the popular Avenida de Mayo
* The Presidential Palace
* The Cathedral and Congress building
* The Pink House which is the President's house
* The Obelisk and next to it the giant BA letters
* The first and large synagogue at 769 Libertad Street
* The famous La Boca neighborhood with colorful houses
* Puerto Madero area which includes luxury restaurants and shops
* San Telmo neighborhood offering a hipster atmosphere with graffiti art - on Sundays the area is especially active with street performances
* The prestigious Recoleta neighborhood and a visit to the magnificent cemetery
* Palermo neighborhood which is a preferred entertainment area
* A visit to the 'Mahane Yehuda' market (click for Patio de los Lecheros post)
* Bosques de Palermo which is a huge green park for walks and a visit to the planetarium
* The famous football games
* Costanera Reserve
* Watching a Tango show
* Casino in the port area
* Horse racing including betting at the Hippodromo (click for post with video)
* Visit to Cafe Tortoni active since 1858
* The huge and especially impressive El Ateneo library
* A show at Teatro Colon
* Visit to the zoo, the Botanical Gardens and the Japanese Gardens (click for post with details)
* Skydiving
* Sailing on the giant Rio Tigre river
* Sailing to Uruguay
* Visit to the nearby coastal town Mar del Plata
