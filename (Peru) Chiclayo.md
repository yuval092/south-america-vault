# Chiclayo

The fourth largest city in Peru.

**Attractions:**
* Municipal Palace of Chiclayo
* Special Mercado Modelo
* San Antonio Church
* Santa Maria Church and Chiclayo Cathedral
* Pimentel Beach
* 'Lord of Sipán' Museum
* Gold Museum
* Tumbas Reales Museum
* Yortuque Sculpture Garden
* Grande Batán Antiquities Site
* Ventarrón Archaeological Center
