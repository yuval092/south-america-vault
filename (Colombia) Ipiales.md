# Ipiales

The border town of Ipiales borders Ecuador. A short drive away is the most beautiful church in Colombia, Las Lajas Sanctuary.
* An especially beautiful church located inside a canyon on a high bridge.
* A worthwhile destination for those crossing the border by land.
