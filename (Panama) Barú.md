# Barú

Near Boquete, considered the highest mountain in the country reaching 3478 meters.

**Activities:**
* You can go on marked day trails, treks and camping
* The climb to the summit takes about 6-8 hours and you can see from it the Pacific and Atlantic Ocean (Caribbean Sea) - breathtaking view

**Trek Info:**
* Can be done independently and is a medium to hard level trek
* There might be clouds hiding the view
* There are shorter hiking trails
* Option for a 4X4 jeep trip to reach the summit to watch the sunrise
