# Fortaleza

Transit city to Jeri or Pipa. Arrival by flight/bus or jeep from Jeri. Features:
* Beautiful beaches like Morro Branco.
* Fishing village and kitesurfing at Cumbuco Beach.
* Beach Park: Largest water park in Latin America (Open daily 10am-5pm; shuttle/taxi from airport available).
