# Potosi

One of the highest cities in the world, standing at an altitude of 4,090 meters. Located 4.5 hours from Uyuni. In the city, you can tour:
* The historic city center.
* Santa Teresa Convent.
* The Gothic Cathedral.
* San Lorenzo Church.
* City observation tower.
* Thermal springs and artificial lakes.
