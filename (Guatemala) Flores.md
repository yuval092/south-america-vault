# Flores

A picturesque and special town lying on Lake Petén Itzá in the northwest of the country and considered a must-visit destination thanks to Tikal National Park.

**Attractions:**
* Tikal National Park is an antiquities site with spectacular and breathtaking pyramids from the Maya period towering above the treetops
* Tour of Jorge's Rope Swing (Roxville beach mentioned in source)
* Kayaking and banana boats in the reserve
* El Mirador viewpoint overlooking the lake
* Tarzan attraction (swing)
* Tubing in the lake
* Boat trip to ARCAS rehabilitation zoo

**Nearby Trips:**
* Departure to Natural Ixpanpajul Park where you can walk on hanging bridges, canopy ziplines
* Tour of Santa Barbara Museum for Maya culture
* Day trip to Actun Can caves
* 5-day trek to El Mirador pyramid
