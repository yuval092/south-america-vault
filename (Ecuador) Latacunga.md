# Latacunga

The second highest volcano in the country and highest active volcano in the world (5800m), located south of Quito and adjacent to Latacunga. In this reserve, you can go on various tours:
* Cotopaxi National Park and climbing the famous volcano.
* Rare Limpiopungo Lagoon tour.
* Rock climbing.
* Bird watching.
* San Agustin Farm near the volcano with Inca ruins.
* Quilotoa Lagoon and Shalala viewpoint.
* Napo River.
* Rio Toachi Canyon.
