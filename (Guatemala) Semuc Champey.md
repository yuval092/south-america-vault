# Semuc Champey

Breathtaking paradise in the center of the country alongside the Cahabón River. Semuc Champey is known as one of the prominent attractions in Guatemala.

**Highlights:**
* 7 natural and tiered water pools spilling into each other in the form of small waterfalls, the pools painted in stunning green-blue and turquoise colors

**Activities:**
* You can trek in the reserve
* Walking trails
* Organized jeep tours
* K'anba water cave tours
* Camping and tent areas
* Arrival at the turquoise water pools
* Tubing
* Impressive pools observation point
* Lanquín caves tour - stalactite caves
* Visit to the Maya tribe ceremony site
