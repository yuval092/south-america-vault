# Boquete

A town located north of David in a rainforest area and Volcán Barú.

**Activities:**
* Marked and independent hiking trails
* Observation point to the Pacific and Atlantic Ocean (Caribbean Sea)
* Tours in coffee plantations and farms
* Three Lost Waterfalls trek in Boquete, 2-3 hours long passing through rainforest
* Quetzales Trail passing through magical rainforest with animal watching
* Pipeline Trail to an impressive waterfall passing through rainforest and bird watching
* Daily rafting in Chiriquí Viejo river
* Horse riding
* Dirt bike riding
* Los Pozos de Caldera hot springs

**Warning:**
Avoid night travel from Boquete to Bocas del Toro, the road is dark, chance of fog and dangerous.
