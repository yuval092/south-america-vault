# Neiva

The desert is located in the southeast of the country near the city of Neiva, west of Cali and Popayán. A spectacular desert as if taken from a movie western where you will find rare landscapes, cacti, and animals. Activities:
* Hiking in the Red Desert (one-hour route and viewpoints).
* Hiking in the Grey Desert (half-hour route ending with a swim in a pool in the desert landscape).
* Visit the observatory to view the Milky Way every evening between 19:00-21:00.
* Dinosaur Museum.
* Trip to Mirador Los Altares and Gigante (giant gorilla and large hand with impressive view).
* Arrival: From Bogotá, Cali, or Armenia to Neiva, then about an hour drive to Villavieja located in the desert area (last jeep leaves at 17:00 from the main square).
* Accommodation: Sleep in Neiva and take tours, sleep in Villavieja, or in the hostel concentration area in the Red Desert.
