# Ilhabela

The largest island in Brazil, 85% of which is a huge nature reserve with dozens of beaches and about 400 waterfalls. A diver's paradise. Features:
* Surrounded by dozens of small islands for diving and sailing visits.
* The small island opposite Pedras Miúdas Beach is an ecological sanctuary for seahorses, starfish, ray eggs, and rare fish.
* Arrival by bus from São Paulo, Rio, or Paraty to São Sebastião, then ferry to the island.
