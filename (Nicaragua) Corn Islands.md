# Corn Islands

2 magical islands on the Caribbean side of Nicaragua, tropical paradise, turquoise sea, white sand, palm trees, and spectacular reefs. The Big Island where the airport is and the Little Island considered younger and cooler. Popular activities are diving, dolphin diving, snorkeling, kayaking, bodyboarding, fishing, sailing around islands, canopy/zip-line, carriage tour in city, yoga. Accessible by flight or public transport - arrive in Managua, take taxi to Mercado Mayoreo, purchase bus to Bluefields (~11 hours drive), taxi from central station to ferry and take ferry to Big Island (departs 09:00 Wed and Sat outbound, Sun and Thu return), sailing time 6 hours. Can cross by boat from Big Island to Little Island.
