# Buena Vista

A small, unique, and beautiful town situated on a hill in the Bolivian lowlands with warm weather year-round. The town offers:
* Its National Park, where travelers can observe many animals, rich vegetation, lagoons, streams, waterfalls, and coffee plantations.
