# Mexico City

Mexico City (Culture) - The capital city with interesting spots to visit such as:
* The main square - Zocalo.
* Zona Rosa area including restaurants, cafes, and shops.
* Must-visit at the breathtaking Teotihuacan pyramids site.
* Visit the impressive National Palace.
* Stroll along Paseo de la Reforma avenue crossing the city.
* Palace of Fine Arts (Bellas Artes).
* Garibaldi Square with Mariachi musicians.
* Stroll in Coyoacan neighborhood and flea market.
* Tour of Inbursa Aquarium.
* San Juan Food Market.
* The spectacular City Cathedral.
* Visit La Ciudadela artisan market.
* Frida Kahlo Museum.
* Tour of Bosque de Chapultepec park where you will find an impressive statue and huge zoo.
* Departure for trek to Nevado de Toluca volcano located about three hours from the city - inactive volcano at 4680m, trek length 7 hours, spectacular view from high altitude.
* Recommended accommodation in Roma, La Condesa, Centro, and Polanco neighborhoods.

Mexico City (Attractions):
* Entrance to the National Stadium.
* Visit the Dolphinarium.
* Six Flags amusement park with crazy roller coasters (check days and hours before arrival, buy online for cheaper price).
* Dinner in the Sky Mexico - floating restaurant.
* Shopping at Antara Mall.
* Madero pedestrian shopping street.
* Sailing in Xochimilco canals alongside dozens of colorful boats - the Venice of Mexico.
* Horseback riding in Rancho Las Cascadas reserve.
* Watching Lucha Libre wrestling matches at Arena Mexico.
* Bullfights every Sunday.
