# Pantanal

The largest wetland area in the world, located near the Amazon. Activities:
* Multi-day tours with accommodation in field cabins.
* Watching capybaras, tarantulas, caimans, birds, piranhas, crocodiles, parrots, monkeys, iguanas, anacondas, jaguars, deer, antelopes, and more.
