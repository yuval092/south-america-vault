# Bonito

A magical town offering tours to jungles and swamps, a large variety of animals and attractions, considered Brazil's natural treasure. Accessible by bus from Campo Grande, Corumbá, or Foz do Iguaçu. Worth visiting:
* Blue Lagoon Cave (Gruta do Lago Azul).
* Rio da Prata river (blue clear water, natural vegetation, and fish).
* Kayaking, tubing, stalactite caves, snorkeling, and more.
