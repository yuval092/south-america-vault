# Cancun

A coastal city known for its golden beaches, vacation atmosphere, and ceaseless energy. In Cancun, you will find:
* Diving and snorkeling courses.
* Clubs, bars, and beach parties, including the famous Coco Bongo.
* Ferry to Isla Mujeres.
* Shopping at Kukulcan Plaza mall.
* Walking around Mercado 28 market.
* Ventura amusement park.
* Surfing.
* Trip to Playa Tortugas.
* Diving to the underwater sculpture museum.
* Swimming with dolphins.
* Trip to Mandala Beach.
* Trip to Maya Park.
* Isla Contoy National Park.
* Water inflatables park.
* Visit to Isla Blanca beach.
* Ferris wheel overlooking the city.
* Cruises to the Caribbean islands.
* Cancun is home to the Caribbean-side airport, with flights to Mexico City, Oaxaca, San Cristobal, and other countries.
