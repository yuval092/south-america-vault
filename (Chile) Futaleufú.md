# Futaleufú

Town offering rafting considered one of the best in the world. Visit:
* Playa Los Lolos: Clear turquoise water, flying fish, 7m jumping rock.
* Piedra del Águila viewpoint: 5km from town, 1-hour climb, overlooking the valley and 2 lagoons.
* Mirador La Blanca.
