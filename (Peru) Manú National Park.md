# Manú National Park

Manu Nature Reserve is considered one of the best reserves for authentic and exciting jungle experience. The Amazon was chosen as one of the seven natural wonders of the world with Peru claiming the crown thanks to Manu.

**Zones & Activities:**
In the reserve you can go to 3 main areas: Reserve Zone, Cultural Zone, National Park where you can go on jungle tours - Princess/Survival, river cruises, viewing rich vegetation, jaguars, crocodiles, monkeys, wild boars, birds and many other animals.

**Recommendation:**
Recommended to visit between 6-7 days and up, 4-5 days tours and survival tours can be done. Arrival to reserve from Puerto Maldonado.
