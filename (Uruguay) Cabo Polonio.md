# Cabo Polonio

Authentic and small fishing village located in southeast of country in middle of special nature reserve with many dunes where you will find yourself in 'relaxation mode' most of time.

**Activities:**
* Walk in main square - 'La Plaza' where you can see stalls and Artists Market
* Tour to Lighthouse and watching sea lions
* Sandboarding in dunes
* Horse riding on coastal avenue
* Whale watching
* Renting beach apartments
* Tour to Santa Teresa Fortress and National Park
* Paragliding, kitesurfing

**Tip:**
Bring mosquito repellent.
