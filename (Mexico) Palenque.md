# Palenque

A city with Mayan history located in the south of the country, also serving as a stopover to/from San Cristobal. Most travelers prefer to sleep in the jungle area outside the city called El Panchan. You can visit:
* The archaeological site and pyramids of the Maya tribe in the jungle - you can climb and enter structures.
* Trip to Agua Azul Lagoon.
* Tour to Misol-Ha Waterfall.
* Raft sailing in Montebello Lagoon.
* Trip to Roberto Barrios Waterfall.
* Visit Palenque Zoo.
