# Mountain Pine Ridge

A nature reserve located in the Maya Mountains, considered one of the most beautiful in Belize. You will find:
* Waterfalls, lakes, and hidden natural water pools.
* Karst caves and hiking trails.
* Sailing on the Macal River.
* A visit to Barton Creek Cave, which can be accessed by canoe.
