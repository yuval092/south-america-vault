# Ica

A desert town known for its vineyard tours and sand dunes where you do buggy and sandboarding.

**Arrival & Transport:**
Arrival by bus from Lima. You can get from Ica to Cusco however it is advisable to return to Lima for a flight or continue on the coastal line via Arequipa. The Ica-Cusco route is dangerous due to route length, dangerous curves and night travel.

**Note:**
Also serves as a transit point to nearby Huacachina which is preferred and more popular. Click here for detailed info on the city.
