# Paracas

A coastal town reachable year-round due to warm weather. Arrival by bus from Lima, Ica or Pisco.

**In the town you can tour:**
* The National Park
* Cruise in Ballestas Islands also known as 'Galapagos for the poor'
* ATV tours
* Paragliding
* Kitesurfing
* Catamaran sailing
* Golden Shadows trek
* 'The Candelabra' viewpoint
* Oasis Moron lagoon and the Red Beach
