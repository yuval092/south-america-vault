# Tupiza

A small city in southern Bolivia with desert scenery, close to the border with Argentina and also serving as a gateway to the Salar salt desert. You can go on:
* Horseback riding tours.
* Trips to impressive canyons and rocky landscapes with a Wild West atmosphere.
