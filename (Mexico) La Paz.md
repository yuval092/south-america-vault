# La Paz

Beautiful city with an impressive boardwalk, restaurants, and breathtaking sunsets at Tecolote Beach. Visit Balandra Beach. City tour to watch sea lions. Visit La Ventana, a small fishing village close to La Paz where you can kitesurf, snorkel with Orca whales, snorkel with sea lions, visit Lighthouse Beach. You can also visit the nearby town of Santiago where there is Sol de Mayo waterfall and Santa Rita hot springs.
