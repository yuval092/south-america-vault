# Tegucigalpa

The capital of Honduras and the largest city in the country, known for the Choluteca River dividing it in two and serving as a transit point to Nicaragua and El Salvador.

**Activities:**
* In the large city you can visit several tourist centers including leaving the city for La Tigra National Park - a nature reserve including walking trails, sleeping in cabins, waterfalls and cloud forests
* Visit to Picacho Park including the Christ statue viewpoint over the city
* Shopping at the large Tegucigalpa mall

**Safety:**
When staying in the city it is advisable to be in the touristy and safe areas.
