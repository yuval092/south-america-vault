# Sucre

A colonial city in the south-central part of the country. Points of interest include:
* The central square Plaza 25 de Mayo.
* The Cathedral with the bell.
* House of Independence.
* Indigenous Textile Art Museum.
* Dinosaur Footprints Park.
* Simon Bolivar Park.
* ATV tours.
* The Villages Trek (medium difficulty, passes through several villages and dinosaur tracks; there are reports of rabid dogs on the route, so go with a guide).
* The Seven Waterfalls.
