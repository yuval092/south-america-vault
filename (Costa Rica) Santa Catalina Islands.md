# Santa Catalina Islands

Located west of Tamarindo, reachable by 20-40 minute boat ride from Tamarindo, Flamingo, Las Catalinas, Brasilito, Conchal, Coco, Papagayo, and Playa Grande. 20 islands formed from volcanic rocks, considered a diver's paradise.
* Underwater life viewing: Exotic fish, sharks, sea turtles, starfish, octopuses, etc.
* Clearest diving period: January to March.
* Snorkeling and catamaran sailing available.
