# Great Blue Hole

A rare underwater world at a depth of 124 meters with giant stalactites 10 meters high.
* Considered one of the most beautiful diving sites in the world.
* The Blue Hole can be reached from Caye Caulker.
* Skydiving into the Blue Hole is available.
