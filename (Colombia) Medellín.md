# Medellín

The second-largest city, considered one of the most modern in the world and vibrant due to its nightlife and surrounding attractions. Also called the City of Eternal Spring thanks to the excellent weather all year round. Points of interest include:
* El Poblado neighborhood and Lleras Park.
* Walking in the city center.
* Pueblito Paisa hill and the impressive view.
* The famous cable car and city view in Arví Park.
* Santa Fe Zoo.
* Pablo Escobar museum and prison tour.
* Comuna 13 - Graffiti tour.
* Paragliding.
* Coffee farm tours.
* Horseback riding tours.
* Bicycle tours.
* Hacienda Nápoles water park.
* Medellín Botanical Garden with a butterfly enclosure.
* Explora Park for science and nature next to the Botanical Garden (includes dinosaur statues).
* Tangaras Bird Reserve.
* El Volador viewpoint over the city.
* Parque Norte amusement park.
* Go-karting.
* Tour to the big rock - Piedra del Peñol.
* Park of Wishes (Parque de los Deseos).
* El Tesoro large shopping center.
* Counterfeit market in Centro Comercial Palacio Nacional.
* Fruit market.
* San Alejo market.
* Visit to El Castillo Museum (a beautiful castle).
* Surroundings: Rio Claro Valley, Guatapé lagoon and famous rock, day trip to 'House in the Air'.
* For a challenging trek: Páramo del Sol (4 hours from Medellín), a medium-level day trek in a magical area.
