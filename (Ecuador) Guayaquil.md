# Guayaquil

Largest city and main port, great stopover. Points of interest:
* Iguana Park in city center.
* Malecon 2000 park and impressive views.
* Bahia Market (electronics).
* Cerro Santa Ana viewpoint.
* Ferris wheel on boardwalk.
* Yuan Heng Buddhist Temple.
* Note: City becomes less pleasant after dark; stay in tourist areas.
