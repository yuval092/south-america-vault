# Tilcara

A picturesque village with an impressive Inca fortress and all the attractions listed under Jujuy. The village serves as a stopover on the way to the border with Bolivia.

**Nearby Attractions:**
Near Tilcara you can find a range of colorful and especially impressive mountains called Serrania de Hornocal located an hour and a quarter north of Tilcara and a quarter of an hour from the city of Humahuaca.
