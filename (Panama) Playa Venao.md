# Playa Venao

A small and charming surfer town. Arrival by bus from the City to Pedasí and from there a taxi to Venao or with a direct shuttle from the City or Bocas del Toro.

**For those looking to:**
* Surf waves
* Kitesurf
* Pamper themselves on the sea in a calm place

**Activities:**
* Yoga
* Deep sea fishing
* Snorkeling
* Diving
* Paddle boats
* Body surfing
* Kayaking
* Trip to Playa Venao waterfall 'Eco Venao' reachable by foot via the beach or main road
* Horse riding
* Animal and bird watching
* Whale watching
* Sea turtle watching (October and November)
* Visit to Pedasí town
* Visit to Isla Iguana (about 20 minutes sail from Pedasí)
