# Maragogi

Beach town near Porto de Galinhas. Locals call it the 'Caribbean of Brazil'. Features:
* Spectacular beaches with clear turquoise water, natural pools, hammocks and swings in the water, water sports, breathtaking scenery.
* Rent a buggy to tour beaches, sail to 3 natural pools for snorkeling, sunset viewpoint.
* Recommended beaches: Antunes (central/crowded with hammocks), Barra Grande (quiet with turquoise water), Ponta de Mangue.
