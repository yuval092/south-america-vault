# Tacna

The southernmost city in Peru serving as a border crossing point to Chile.

**In the city you can visit:**
* Main Square
* Museums
* Churches
* City center market
* Tour of Petroglifos reserve
* Boca del Rio beach
* Villa Fortuna Casino
