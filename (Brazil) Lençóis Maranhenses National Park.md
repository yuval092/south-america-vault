# Lençóis Maranhenses National Park

National park consisting entirely of sand dunes and hundreds of lakes creating a spectacular look. One of Brazil's most beautiful yet complex/expensive destinations. Activities:
* Hiking, treks, jeep tours, private flight over lakes.
* Arrival: Direct jeep from Jeri, or jeep to Jijoca then bus to Barreirinhas, or flight to São Luís then travel to Barreirinhas.
* Best time: May to September (check if lakes are full beforehand).
