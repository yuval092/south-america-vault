# Leticia

The southernmost port city in the country, lying along the Amazon River, characterized by the border triangle with Peru and Brazil. Serves as a border crossing and hub for Amazon jungle tours. Recommendations:
* Amacayacu National Park tour.
* Tanimboca Natural Reserve.
* Ecological park.
* Monkey Island.
* Bioparque Etuena (animal center).
* Victoria Regia garden (huge flower).
* Visit the main square at sunset (hundreds of birds arrive).
* Visit local tribes.
* Jungle tours (princess/survival, lodge accommodation, animal watching).
* Boat crossing to Brazil (Tabatinga) and Peru (Iquitos) available.
