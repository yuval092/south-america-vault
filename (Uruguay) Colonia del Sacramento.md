# Colonia del Sacramento

Picturesque and magical town sitting on bank of Rio de la Plata river.

**Highlights:**
* Main attraction is Historic Quarter with buildings and streets from colonial period. Streets paved with stones giving it special look.
* City Lighthouse
* City Hall Clock
* Main Square
* Markets
* Oldest church in Uruguay Iglesia Matriz
* 8 museums
* Trip on promenade
* Ferrando Beach and Anchorena Park
