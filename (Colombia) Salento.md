# Salento

A small, magical, and beautiful village in the heart of the famous coffee region. From the tropical village, you can reach several points of interest:
* Cocora Valley tour.
* Visit to coffee farms.
* Guided city tour.
* Coffee Valley waterfalls.
* La Rivera Park.
* Cupping sites.
* Kasaguadua Natural Reserve.
* Jeep tours.
* Alto de la Cruz viewpoint.
* Horseback riding.
* Paragliding.
* Hot springs in Santa Teresa.
* Trip to Machín Volcano.
* Trek to Tolima peak in Los Nevados National Park (park has several volcanoes and snowy peaks).
* Trips to the snowy mountains Paramillo and Cisne.
* Visit to the picturesque town of Filandia.
* Visit to the Botanical Garden.
* Santa Rita Park with several waterfalls and hiking trails.
* The amazing La Carbonera Valley with whole forests of the famous palm trees.
* About an hour from Salento is Parque del Café - the largest amusement park in Colombia.
