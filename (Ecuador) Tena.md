# Tena

Small tropical town lying along the Tena River in the heart of rainforests. Serves as departure point for:
* Jungle tours in Amazon, Cuyabeno, and Yasuni reserves.
* Excellent rafting.
* Visit to 'La Isla' Park.
* Bridge overlooking the river.
* Scenic Tena boardwalk.
* Day trip in town's central park.
* Jungle tours in Cuyabeno and La Paia reserves.
* San Rafael Waterfall tour.
* Laguna Azul.
* Limoncocha Lake.
* Rafting/Kayaking on Jondachi and Jatunyacu rivers.
* Endangered animals center.
* Jumandy Cave tour.
* San Jacinto waterfalls.
* Tamia Yura farm.
* Visit Misahualli village (free monkeys and indigenous tribes).
