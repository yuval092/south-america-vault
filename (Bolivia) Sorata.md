# Sorata

A pastoral town situated next to an impressive green mountainous area. Israelis who visited defined it as the paradise of Bolivia. The town combines tours and treks:
* The Gold Way.
* The Lagoons Trek.
* Illampu Trek.
* Daily trip to San Pedro Cave.
* Horseback riding and cycling tours.
