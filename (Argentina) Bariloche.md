# Bariloche

The most important destination in Argentina for the Israeli traveler and the end/start point of the Carretera Austral trip (in an organized van) and a crossing point to Pucón in Chile. It can be reached as part of the route by flight or buses from Ushuaia, Calafate, Buenos Aires.

**Overview:**
An impressive city situated on a lake and surrounded by mountains, offering plenty of trips and attractions and lots of meat.

**Central Attractions:**
* Viewpoint over the city and the lake at Kilometer 17 which can be done by cable car
* Another viewpoint at Kilometer 5
* Black Lagoon trek (click for info post) and Frey Jacob trek taking 3 days (click for detailed details on treks)
* Short daily trek to Cerro La Buitrera (click for post with details and photos)
* Daily trip to Los Pozones (click for post with details and photos)
* The Black Glacier and Cerro Tronador 'The Furious Mountain' (click for Otto Meiling trek)
* Cruise and Los Cantaros falls
* The famous Seven Lakes route on the way to Pucón (see details below)
* Horse riding in the nearby mountains (click for horse tours)
* Tour and famous Asado meal of Jorge (click for tour)
* Chocolate Museum
* Visit to Bambi's Forest which looks like a forest from fairytales (Sendero de los Arrayanes)
* Dinosaur Park where you will find giant dinosaur models in the forest (click for website)
* Piedras Blancas adventure sports and rope park (click for website)

**When to visit:**
The season is November to April inclusive, May is still a good month to visit, June to October is winter and there are winter attractions where skiing is the main attraction.
