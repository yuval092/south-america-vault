# Salar de Uyuni

A small and partly desolate town in southern Bolivia serving as a departure point for the famous Salar de Uyuni salt desert. In the Salar, you will find:
* Geysers, lagoons, and amazing landscapes.
* The Salar de Uyuni is considered a must-visit spot in the country.
