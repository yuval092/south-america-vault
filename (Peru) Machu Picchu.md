# Machu Picchu

Considered the most important archaeological site in the continent and a must-destination for every traveler in South America and Peru specifically. The Lost City of the Incas and the famous Inca Trail trek.

**Tickets & Arrival:**
* You can purchase entrance ticket to the site at Historical Museum offices in Cusco or in Aguas Calientes.
* Arrival from Cusco to Aguas Calientes by train, car or foot combined with bus however the train is the safest option and prefer it.
* Arrival from Aguas Calientes to Machu Picchu by bus.
