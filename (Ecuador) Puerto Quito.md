# Puerto Quito

Small village west of Quito known for its tropical warm weather, surrounded by rivers, waterfalls, and rich vegetation. From the quiet village you can tour:
* Caoni and Silanche rivers.
* El Canton (bird watching, butterflies, vegetation).
* Blue Waterfall tour.
* Grupo Mieles Reserve and High Waterfall.
* Escondida Waterfall in the heart of vegetation.
* Fruit and cocoa farms.
