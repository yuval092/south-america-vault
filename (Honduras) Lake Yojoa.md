# Lake Yojoa

A quiet town from which you depart for a green nature reserve in northwest Honduras including rich vegetation alongside impressive mountains and a spectacular lagoon.

**In the lake reserve you can reach several interesting places:**
* Trip to Pulhapanzak Waterfalls including zipline and dipping in the waterfall pools
* Sailing on the lake
* Tour of Cerro Azul Meambar National Park
* Hiking trails
* Tour of Taulabe stalactite caves
* Kayaking in Los Naranjos Ecological Park
