# Copacabana

A town located on the popular Lake Titicaca, serving as a border crossing between Bolivia and Peru. From the settlement, you can go on tours to:
* The Floating Islands.
* Isla del Sol (Island of the Sun) - an archaeological site.
* Isla de la Luna (Island of the Moon).
* Copacabana Beach.
* The Municipal Cathedral.
* The Poncho Museum.
* Stone Pillars tour.
* Yampupata Trek.
* Cerro Calvario viewpoint.
