# Chichicastenango

A picturesque town in the mountains, surrounded by valleys with a surprising attraction - the largest market in the country.

**Market Info:**
* The market is active on Thursdays and Sundays and is especially colorful
* Includes clothes, shoes, souvenirs, jewelry, pictures, fruits and vegetables, meat and more
* The only rule that applies here is bargaining
* For those who love markets this is a unique experience
