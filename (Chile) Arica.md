# Arica

Border town with Peru, considered one of the best surf spots in the continent. Visit:
* Lauca National Park (unique desert landscape resembling Mars).
* Las Vicuñas Nature Reserve and Salar de Surire.
* Lake Chungará.
* Parinacota Volcano (4600m high).
* Morro de Arica (big rock).
* The beach.
