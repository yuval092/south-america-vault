# Cozumel

A beautiful Caribbean island located east of Playa del Carmen and known as a water sports site. Access is via a short ferry from Playa del Carmen or flight to the island's airport (CZM). On the island, you will find:
* Diving and snorkeling of all kinds.
* Chabad House.
* Visit to the Dolphin Park.
* Kayaking.
* Buggy rentals.
* Trip to Punta Sur Park.
* Flyboard attraction.
* Jet skis.
* Guided city tour.
* Spa and massages.
* Reef sailing to the spectacular Palancar and Colombia reefs.
* Tequila tour.
* ATV tours.
* Visit to Playa Paraiso.
* Sailing and snorkeling at El Cielo where you will find giant and colorful starfish.
* Chill and attractions at Isla Pasion (small island north of Cozumel full of attractions).
* Tour of Mayan archaeological site.
* Restaurants.
* Inflatables park at Playa Mia.
* Horseback riding tours.
* Visit to Palancar Beach.
* Tour of a cocoa production factory.
* Visit to the Pueblo del Maiz indigenous village.
* Unique attraction for coral restoration using Minecraft structures, which you can dive to with diving masks.
