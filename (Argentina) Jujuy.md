# Jujuy

A city located near Salta offering desert landscapes and mountain ranges very reminiscent of the Salar in Bolivia.

**Attractions:**
* Besides visiting the city you can visit the hot springs
* Potrero de Yala Nature Reserve
* Quebrada de Humahuaca offering rural trips
* The spectacular Seven Colors Mountain (arrival by car about two and a half hours drive to Humahuaca)
* Salinas Grandes Salt Flat which reminds very much of the Salar in Bolivia and is considered the second largest in the world (see details below)
* Laguna de Guayatayoc with pink flamingos
* A cave with ancient wall paintings in the village of Huachichocana
* Trip to a giant waterfall called La Garganta del Diablo
* Calilegua National Park with spectacular mountains and wild animals
