# Mazunte

A small hippie town located east of Puerto Escondido, about an hour's drive. Packed with restaurants and stalls, secluded beaches. Worth visiting Zipolite beach which is somewhat reminiscent of the Sinai atmosphere. Note that nudity is allowed on this beach, so don't be surprised.
