# San Cristobal de las Casas

A fascinating and special city near the Guatemala border, situated among ancient and authentic villages. It sits between the Caribbean and Pacific sides and serves as a crossing point. Accessible by bus from Bacalar or Cancun, or by flight. In the city, you can visit:
* The city square - Plaza 31 de Marzo.
* Shopping at San Cristobal Market.
* Lagos de Colon (Colon Lakes) complex.
* Trip to Cenote Chucumaltik and snorkeling.
* Las Nubes reserve - sailing and camping by the lagoon.
* Village tours in Chiapas.
* Tour of Sumidero Canyon National Park and river boat ride.
* Trip to Arcotete Park and cave tour.
* Climb to the church viewpoint overlooking the city.
* Trip to Miramar Lagoon and lake sailing.
* Visit to Moxviquil Orchid Garden.
* Trip to El Chiflon waterfalls.
* Trip to Palenque archaeological site from the Mayan period.
* Visit to Tonina Pyramid, considered the tallest pyramid in the world, located about 3 hours drive away.
* Trip to Agua Azul lagoons and camping.
* Trip to Montebello Lagoons and National Park (3 hours drive), offering visits to 59 lagoons and Mayan ruins.
* Visit and trek to Chichonal Volcano located 6-7 hours from the city (can be booked including accommodation from San Cristobal).
