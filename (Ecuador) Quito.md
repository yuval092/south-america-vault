# Quito

Capital of Ecuador located in the north-central part of the country. The historic city offers a mix of modernization and antiquity inside and out. In the city, you can visit:
* Independence Square (Plaza de la Independencia).
* Presidential Palace.
* La Alameda Park.
* Temple of Light Museum.
* Equator Museum.
* Historic Center (Old City).
* Winged Virgin Statue (El Panecillo) - giant statue of Virgin Mary.
* Panecillo Hill viewpoint overlooking the city.
* Famous La Ronda street.
* Scala Shopping Center.
* Large Quicentro Mall.
* Artisan Market.
