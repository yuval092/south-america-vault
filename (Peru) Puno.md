# Puno

A city located alongside the beautiful Lake Titicaca and serves as a border crossing city between Peru and Bolivia. Arrival by bus from Arequipa or Cusco as well as flight to nearby Juliaca.

**Attractions:**
* Tour of Floating Islands (Uros) in Lake Titicaca - you can sleep in the islands at one of the families in 'Uros' island and during stay receive traditional clothes and eat local dinner (phone without WhatsApp for family on island - Felix 981932917)
* Visit and accommodation on Amantaní Island
* Tour of authentic Taquile Island
* Cemetery in Sillustani peninsula
* Sillustani towers which are an engineering wonder
* Condor Wasi viewpoint overlooking the city
* Hospitality in pastoral Llachón Chico village
