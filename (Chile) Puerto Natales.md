# Puerto Natales

Important town as the access point to Torres del Paine National Park. Details:
* Famous treks like the W Trek.
* Park area ~2000 sq km including snowy mountains, lakes, glaciers.
* Important: For park accommodation, book weeks in advance, or check availability on site, or settle for day trips.
* Arrival from Punta Arenas, El Calafate (Argentina), or flight within Chile (e.g., end of Carretera Austral).
