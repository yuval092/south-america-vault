# Oruro

A city unique for its carnival and the second-largest salt desert in the country. From the city, you can tour:
* Salar de Coipasa salt desert.
* Lake Poopo.
* Thermal baths site.
* Sajama National Park.
* Plaza de Oruro and its markets.
* The city's Culture Museum.
* Yarvicoya Church (recognized as a national monument).
* San Miguel Museum.
* Mining Museum dedicated to mine workers.
* Cala Cala archaeological site for ancient paintings.
* The Devil's Carnival takes place close to the Brazilian carnival, considered one of the largest in the continent. No need to buy tickets in advance; prices range from 100 to 900 Bolivianos. It is very important to book accommodation in advance.
