# Salvador

Third largest city, origin of Samba and Capoeira, known for the world's largest street carnival (free). Transit point to Morro and Chapada. Points of interest:
* Pelourinho (historic district).
* Barra district and famous lighthouse.
* Porto da Barra beach.
* Water sports: Jet ski, SUP, kayaks.
* Visit Praia do Forte (1.5 hours away, beautiful beach with sea turtle reserve).
