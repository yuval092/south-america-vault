# Colón Island

A group of magical islands on the border of Panama and Costa Rica. A must-visit destination in Panama.

**Arrival:**
Arrival by shuttle or bus combined with boat from the City, Playa Venao or Puerto Viejo in Costa Rica (see border crossings above the map).

**Islands & Activities:**
* The central island where you sleep is Colón
* Visit nearby islands - Zapatilla Island including starfish, sloths and dolphins
* Red Frog Island
* Trips and attractions
* Snorkeling
* Watching plankton
* Small frogs in rare colors
* Famous starfish at Starfish Beach
* Horse riding
* Surfing waves
* Dolphin tour

**Nightlife:**
Every Friday there is a Filthy Friday electronic party - 'Three tropical islands, one crazy party in paradise' (check Filthy Friday website for updates).
