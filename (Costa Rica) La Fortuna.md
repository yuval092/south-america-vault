# La Fortuna

Small town in the north, especially popular for the amount of activities nearby. Activities include:
* Climbing Arenal Volcano.
* Sailing on Lake Arenal.
* 'Arenal 1968' Trek (Arenal trail and Lava trail).
* Horseback riding to Arenal Volcano.
* La Fortuna Waterfall.
* Tabacon Hot Springs (look for free springs along the river too).
* Cycling.
* Baldi complex (pools and waterfalls).
* El Salto (swim in river with waterfalls and Tarzan swing).
* Tour of Arenal National Site.
* Chocolate tour.
* Waterfall rappelling.
* Professional rafting on Rio Toro.
* 2-hour hike to Rio Celeste (river with special light blue color).
* Tour to Chato Volcano.
* Chollin River and hot springs.
* Arenal Ecological Park.
* Proyecto Asis (animal sanctuary).
* Canopy zip-line between trees.
* El Silencio viewpoint.
* Venado Caves tour.
* Bogarin Trail (mini jungle tour seeing frogs, sloths, toucans, special trees).
