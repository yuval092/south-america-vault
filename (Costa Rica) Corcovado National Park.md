# Corcovado National Park

Puerto Jimenez is a quiet town serving as a departure point for Corcovado National Park, with spectacular beaches and luxury resorts. Can also stay in nearby Drake Bay. Corcovado is a true paradise for nature lovers (most biologically diverse place in the world per Nat Geo).
* Tours watching animals: Monkeys, wild boars, tapirs, small crocodiles, iguanas, birds, pumas (with luck).
* Floating Rio Claro tour and snorkeling.
* Recommended to take guides.
* Recommended station: Sirena (18km walk or boat from Puerto Jimenez/Drake Bay).
* Note: No food allowed in reserve (except dry/closed products). Prepare with food provided by guide.
