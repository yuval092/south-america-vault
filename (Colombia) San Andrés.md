# San Andrés

A beautiful island with a tropical climate located northwest of Colombia in the Caribbean Sea. In the resort island, you can visit:
* Ecological Park.
* Cueva de Morgan (Pirate history tour).
* Pirate souvenir salon.
* Island amusement park.
* Big Pond Lagoon.
* Botanical Garden.
* Sailing in Bahía del Cove.
* Sailing to Cayo Sucre and Johnny Cay Park.
* Sailing to Cayo Córdoba and Aquarium tour.
* Sea tour to Cayo Santander reserve.
* Trip to abandoned pirate ship.
* Diving to Poseidon statue.
* San Luis beach.
* Rocky Cay and Sound Bay beaches.
* Sunset near West View.
* Important: Exit ticket required (buy round trip or prepare dummy ticket). Tourist tax: $25.
