# San José Del Guaviare

A town located south of Bogotá, 11 hours by bus. The town offers jungles, animals, and especially kind people. The place is untouristed and off the beaten track. Attractions:
* Laguna Negra (lake sailing, bird watching, vegetation emerging from lake).
* Giant rocks at La Lindosa.
* Los Tuneles (tunnels like in Indiana Jones).
* Caño Rosado (river of colors).
* Puerta de Orión (giant rock).
* Meeting indigenous tribes.
* City of Rocks.
* 1000-year-old rock paintings and caves.
* Macarena National Park visit.
* Rio Guaviare river.
* Natural hot springs.
* Swimming with dolphins.
