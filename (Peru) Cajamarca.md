# Cajamarca

The city is located in Peru's northern center enjoying summer weather most of the year.

**Activities:**
* Visit Kuntur Wasi Archaeological Center
* Inca Hot Springs
* Santa Apolonia Hill overlooking city
* San Nicolas Lagoon
* Pastoral Cumbemayo Trek
* Callacpuma Reserve tour
