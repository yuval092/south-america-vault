# Tucumán

A city in northern Argentina serving as a stopover on the way to Salta. Known for the nature areas in its surroundings.

**In the city you can visit:**
* Several points of interest including a tour of the pedestrian street and Independence Square - Plaza Independencia and the historic pedestrian street
* Shopping at Mercado del Norte market
* Trip to 9 de Julio Park and viewing Lake San Miguel
* Visit to the Folklore Museum
* Visit to Lake Cadillal where you will find extreme sports
* Viewing the impressive Rio Noque waterfall
* Visit to the nearby Tafi del Valle village
* Tour of the colorful village Villa Nougués
* Termas de Rio Hondo hot springs
* Treks in Los Alisos National Park
