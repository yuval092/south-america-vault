# Guatemala City

The capital and largest city in the country.

**Activities:**
* Visit the main Plaza Mayor
* Central Market
* La Aurora Zoo
* Tour of the Historic Center and the Cathedral
* Tour of the Mapa en Relieve (Relief Map) site
* Grados Norte restaurant complex
* Shopping at the large malls Oakland and Arcadia
* Casinos
* Trip to Lake Amatitlán
* X-Park where you can do extreme sports
* Walk around the Cayalá shopping and entertainment complex which has an amusement park, paintball, bars, casinos, bowling, and many restaurants
