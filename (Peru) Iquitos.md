# Iquitos

City in northern Peru located on Amazon River.

**From here you can:**
* Go on regular and survival trips in jungle, Pacaya Samiria
* Cross border by sailing to Colombia and Brazil
* San Pedro ceremony

**In the city:**
* Visit Zoo and Quistococha Botanical Garden
* Jungla natural pool
* 'Centro de Rescate Amaz√≥nico' center where you can feed manatees
* Visit Boulevard boardwalk
* Worth visiting Al Frio y Al Fuego restaurant located on water reachable by boat with pampering pool (click for details)
* Visit Santa Clara village about quarter hour from city with beach on Amazon River

**Arrival:**
Flight from Lima or sailing from Pucallpa (slow ferry of 5 days to week), sailing from Yurimaguas (arrival to Tarapoto, from there bus two hours to Yurimaguas, sailing of 15-20 hours to Nauta and from there taxi of hour and half to Iquitos), sailing from Leticia Colombia or Tabatinga Brazil.
