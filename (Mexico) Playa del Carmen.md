# Playa del Carmen

A vibrant and especially popular Caribbean beach town with rich and stormy nightlife. Located about an hour from Cancun and Tulum. From here you can go to:
* The Pink Lakes (Las Coloradas).
* Bel-Air Animal Park.
* Ferry to Cozumel island.
* Trip to Holbox, Bacalar Lagoon, and Tulum.
* Swimming with sea turtles in Akumal.
* Horseback riding tours in the jungle.
* Chichen Itza pyramid site.
* Quinta Alegria shopping complex.
* Paseo del Carmen shopping complex.
* Skydiving.
* Fishing trips.
* Tour of Rio Secreto stalactite caves.
* Xcaret Park in the jungle.
* Xel-Ha water park.
* Xplor Park.
* Punta Venado - bike riding park.
* Jeep, buggy, and ATV tours.
* The famous Coco Bongo club.
* Cenote tours.
* Diving and snorkeling sites.
* Swimming with dolphins.
* Walking on the beautiful promenade.
* Swimwear stores.
* Strolling on the main street Quinta Avenida full of restaurants and cafes.
* Bird park.
