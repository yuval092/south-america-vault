# Rosario Islands

Virgin and magical islands 45 minutes by boat from Cartagena. Isla Grande is the main island; best to reach Playa Libre where hostels are located. Features:
* High-level hotels and various hostels.
* Tip: Bring food and drink.
* Nightlife, restaurants, kayaking.
* Snorkeling for spectacular corals.
* Glowing lagoons.
* Sailing to Isla Arena (starfish).
* Visit Pablo Escobar's estate and plane.
