# Taganga

A small and peaceful fishing village near Santa Marta in the north, attracting many travelers due to its location on the coast and fame as a diving site. Activities:
* Diving of all kinds and snorkeling.
* Walking the boardwalk.
* Relaxing at Playa Taganga.
* Trip to Tayrona Park.
* Lost City Trek.
* Palomino National Park visit.
* Beaches: Playa Grande and Playa Cristal.
