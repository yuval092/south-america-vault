# Baños

Small town next to an active volcano, named after nearby springs. Located 3.5 hours from Quito, known as capital of attractions:
* San Martin Zoo.
* Beautiful Virgin Waterfall.
* Virgin Viewpoint trip.
* Cross Viewpoint trip.
* Climb Tungurahua Volcano.
* Santa Clara Springs.
* Ojos del Volcan viewpoint.
* El Salado and Virgin hot springs.
* Cable car at Pastaza mountain.
* Sangay National Park.
* Paseo de los Monos (Monkey Park) in Puyo.
* Cuyabeno Reserve.
* Bungee jumping from bridges.
* Waterfall rappelling.
* Paragliding.
* Casa del Arbol (Tree House and famous swing) tour.
* Rafting.
* Rock climbing.
* Descent to Pailón del Diablo waterfall.
