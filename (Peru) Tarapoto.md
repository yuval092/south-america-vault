# Tarapoto

Tropical town located deep in rainforests and alongside Amazon River estuary offering unique attractions.

**Activities:**
* Jungle tours and sleeping in cabins
* Water caves
* Boat sailing in river
* Waterfall rappelling
* Ziplines
* Stalactite cave tour
* Pucayaquillo road tour
* Ahuashiyacu Waterfalls tour
* Sauce Lagoon
* Yacupark small water park

**Nearby:**
About an hour from town, Rioja waterfall second largest in world in river spilling from it you can do tubing and swimming. Tour to nearby Moyobamba town where you can dip in hot springs. Laguna Azul - about two hours from Tarapoto.
