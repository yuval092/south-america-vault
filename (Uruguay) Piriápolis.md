# Piriápolis

Beautiful resort town located near Punta del Este.

**In the calm town you can reach several points of interest:**
* San Antonio Hill and impressive viewpoint
* Touristy beaches Playa Hermosa and Playa San Antonio
* Walking on pleasant town promenade
* Tour in Piria's Castle
* Pastoral La Cascada Park
* Climbing Pan de Azúcar Mountain and watching animals and view of area
* Tour in Archaeological Park
* Nature and Animal Reserve - La Estación de Cría de Fauna
