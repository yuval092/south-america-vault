# Placencia

A village in southern Belize offering spectacular Caribbean beaches, coconut trees, snorkeling, diving, and lagoon kayaking. Nearby:
* North of the village, you will find Maya Beach with luxurious resorts.
* From here, you can reach the Monkey River to find crocodiles, monkeys, and other animals.
