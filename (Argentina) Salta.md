# Salta

Serves as a transit point on the way to Bolivia but also offers an impressive city in an area abundant with trips.

**Attractions:**
* You can visit Mount San Bernardo by cable car
* Visit Cachi town made entirely of stone
* The Train to the Clouds route which includes huge tracks and bridges in the Andes mountains and is an impressive attraction (click for official Train to the Clouds website)
* Visit Lake Cabra Corral
