# Foz do Iguaçu

One of the most important attractions is the Iguazu Falls. Details:
* The Brazilian side visit is shorter than the Argentine side (2-3 hours).
* Activities: Sailing to falls, bike tours, rafting, helicopter flight, jungle jeep tour (Poço Preto - can be done on foot or bike), Three Borders Landmark.
* Itaipu Dam (huge and impressive attraction).
* Bird Park.
* Ciudad del Este in Paraguay is worth visiting for the free-trade market (electronics focus).
* Open daily (closed Mondays) 09:00-18:00, entry until 16:30.
