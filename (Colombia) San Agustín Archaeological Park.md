# San Agustín Archaeological Park

Popayán is a lovely town with Baroque-style houses painted white. It serves as a departure point to one of the most important archaeological sites in Colombia in the village of San Agustín. Features:
* 24 concentrations of statues (over 500 total) of a strange culture with hundreds of impressive statues carved in rock, alongside burial sites and places of worship.
* In San Agustín, you can go on a 10km day trip towards La Estrecho del Magdalena (beautiful rocks and swimming).
* Cinco Waterfall (one hour walk from town).
* Chaquira viewpoint over a canyon and small statue (1.5 hours walk).
* Horseback riding, rafting on Magdalena River, jeep tours, local market.
