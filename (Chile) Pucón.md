# Pucón

Chile's capital of attractions and trips. Accessible by bus from Santiago and Bariloche. Offers:
* Climbing the active Villarrica Volcano.
* Tubing, waterfall rappelling, rafting, body rafting.
* Hot springs.
* Ojos del Caburgua waterfalls.
* Ants Trek in a beautiful reserve and lake visits.
* Villarrica Traverse Trek (3-6 days passing lagoons and volcanoes).
* El Cañi private reserve (daily trek to 1500m with 360-degree view of volcanoes and lakes).
* San Sebastian Trek in Huerquehue Reserve (one of Chile's most beautiful, 1200m high).
* El Mandarino stream (clear water, swimming).
* Natural pools surrounded by basalt rocks nearby (taxi drivers know the spot).
