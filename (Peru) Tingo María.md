# Tingo María

A cute town in the heart of the jungle located west of Huaraz and above Oxapampa.

**Highlights:**
* Offers mountainous Amazon, streams, waterfalls, caves, warm weather
* Destination off the beaten track and especially cheap

**Arrival & Activities:**
Can arrive by flight from Lima or bus to Huánuco and from there to Tingo María. Can do waterfall rappelling, visit Owl Cave (impressive stalactite cave), Alfura natural pool, Jacintillo viewpoint, and the town's famous waterfalls. Click for hostel recommendation in Tingo.
