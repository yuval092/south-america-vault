# Urubici

A small town about two hours drive from Florianópolis, in an area abundant with mountains and waterfalls. Attractions:
* Corvo Branco cliffs (50 km steep dirt road route to rainforests and waterfalls).
* Morro da Igreja (Church Hill) at 1,800m height; wind and water carved a church-shaped cave in the rock with ocean views (free registration at ICBIM nature reserve required beforehand).
* Seven Waterfalls River (5-hour medium difficulty comfortable hike through jungle and seven waterfalls).
* Avencal Waterfall (100m high) and upper waterfall (120m high, zip-line available).
* Morro do Campestre viewpoint with unique rocks.
* São Joaquim National Park visit.
* Vast green areas, amazing scenery, free-roaming horses, firefly watching at night.
