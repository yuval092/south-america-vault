# Cockscomb Basin Wildlife Sanctuary Parking

A reserve rare in its beauty, considered the first jaguar preservation site in the world. You will find:
* About 50 jaguars.
* Waterfalls and mountains with spectacular views.
* Hiking trails and river tubing.
* A huge variety of plants, insects, birds, furry animals, and other diverse wildlife.
