# Oxapampa

Mountainous Amazon and breathtaking nature reserve located about 10 hours by bus from Lima (or flight) and characterized as one of the reserves belonging to Amazon alongside Andes mountains.

**Activities:**
From Oxapampa town you can go to the reserve and conduct jungle tours, river cruises, enter National Park, fishing experience, cave viewing, climbing and ziplines, viewing many animals, diverse vegetation, waterfalls, local villages, lakes, jeep tours and guided tours.
