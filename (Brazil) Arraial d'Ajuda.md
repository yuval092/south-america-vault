# Arraial d'Ajuda

Amazing beach strip, formerly more popular than Morro/Jeri/Ilha Grande. Arrival by ferry/taxi from Porto Seguro. Features:
* Pristine beaches, invested and pampering Pousadas, trips, and addictive Brazilian atmosphere.
* One hour drive to Pau Brasil National Park.
