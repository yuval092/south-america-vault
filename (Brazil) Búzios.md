# Búzios

Located about three hours from Rio, offering beautiful beaches, blue water, and white sand. Highlights include:
* Popular beach: Praia João Fernandes.
* Known by locals as the 'Ibiza of Brazil' due to rich nightlife with clubs like Privilege, Silk, and Café de la Musique.
* Attractions: Buggy rides, yacht sailing, surfing of all kinds, jet skiing, and more.
* Rent a villa with a pool and indulge like in the movies.
* Ferradurinha Beach where you can find giant turtles.
