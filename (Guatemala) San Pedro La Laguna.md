# San Pedro La Laguna

A beautiful town sitting on the pastoral Lake Atitlán and a magnet for many tourists thanks to the fascinating view and sequence of activities.

**From the calm town you can go to several points of interest:**
* Cruise in Laguna Atitlán
* Market and stall tour in the town (Santiago Market)
* Tour to San Pedro Volcano
* Horse riding tours
* Viewpoint over the lagoon and the Indian Nose (mountain that looks like an Indian nose)
* ATV tours
* Dirt bike tours
* Banana boat on the lake
* Paragliding over the lake
* SUP lesson in the lagoon
* Cliff jumping with a parachute
* Jet skis
* SUP on the lake
* Coffee plantation tours
* Kayaking in the lagoon
* Visit to Finca Beach which is a beautiful beach with black sand
* Climb to the Gold Viewpoint overlooking the lake
* Yoga classes and massages in the nearby town San Marcos
* Cooking workshops
* Painting workshop
* Goldsmithing workshop
* Trip to Santiago town and watching the Maya natives
* Tour to the nearby village Santa Cruz and climbing the volcanoes
* Arrival at Panajachel and tour of the market and butterfly/monkey park
* Walking tour to Tzununa alongside the lagoon
* Trip to the nearby village Chichi and visiting the colorful market
