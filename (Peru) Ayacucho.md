# Ayacucho

A town located on the way between Lima and Cusco offering a visit to spectacular natural pools resembling Semuc Champey in Guatemala.

**Tips:**
* It is advisable to arrive at early noon so the sun is positioned over the pools and the color becomes spectacular turquoise
* About 10 hours from Lima and Cusco, about 7 hours from Ica, can be reached by flight
