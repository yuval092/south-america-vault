# Tumbes

Northernmost city in Peru touching Ecuador border.

**In the summer city you can visit:**
* Acapulco Beach
* Whale Island and whale watching area
* Punta Sal Beach Reserve
* Green Puerto Pizarro Nature Reserve
* Cabeza De Vaca Archaeological Center
* Cerros de Amotape National Park
* Palo Santo Ecological Park
* Crocodile Zoo
