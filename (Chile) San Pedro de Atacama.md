# San Pedro de Atacama

Departure point for the Salar (famous salt desert, 3rd largest in world). Arrival from nearby Calama. Tours go to Bolivian Salar and back or continue to Uyuni. Attractions:
* World's highest geysers.
* Red Rocks.
* Rainbow Valley.
* Moon Valley.
* Stargazing (best spot in the world).
* Puritama Hot Springs.
