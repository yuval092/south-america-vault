# Chapada dos Veadeiros National Park

Located about 6 hours west of Salvador. Main attraction is Chapada Diamantina National Park. Features:
* Treks, trips, lakes, swamps, caves, waterfalls, spectacular landscapes unique to this area.
* Considered one of the most beautiful places in Brazil.
* Reserve is larger than Holland, Belgium, and Switzerland combined.
* Attractions: Longest cave in the world, Fumaca Waterfall (2nd highest in world).
* Ribeirão do Meio pools (reddish water, natural slide rock).
* Poço Azul (Blue Cave with clear water).
* Stalactite caves.
* Pai Inácio Mountain viewpoint.
* Devil's Waterfall trail.
* Pratinha (zip-line and snorkeling with turtles).
* Cachoeira do Buracão (waterfall inside beautiful canyon with swimming).
