# Palomino

A small village about two hours from Santa Marta and Taganga, offering spectacular nature and views, with few Israelis and tourists. You will find restaurants, cafes, smoothie stands, and bars with good atmosphere. Visit:
* Las Gaviotas and Rio Palomino beaches.
* Tubing attraction at Pozo Caiman.
* Visit Don Diego fishing village.
* Visit indigenous tribes.
