# Cartagena

Port city in northern Colombia on the Caribbean coast, known as a colorful city with warm weather and vacation atmosphere, considered one of the most visited cities with rich nightlife. Highlights:
* Popular neighborhoods: Laguito, Bocagrande.
* Old City: Getsemaní neighborhood, Ancient Quarter, Main Cathedral.
* San Felipe Fortress tour.
* Dolphin show at Oceanario.
* Sailing to paradise islands (Isla Grande and Rosario Islands).
* Visit the rare Playa Blanca.
* Luxury cruises and transit to Panama.
* Sailing to Casa en el Agua (house in the middle of the sea).
* Diving and snorkeling.
* Totumo Volcano (mud bath tour).
* Jet skis.
* Beautiful beach strip: Miramar, Boquilla, Punta Arena.
* Barú beach area (virgin beaches, cabin accommodation) is 1 hour away.
* Note: For travel to Santa Marta, choose bus lines (Berlinas/Marsol) passing on the day route, not through villages, due to robbery risk.
