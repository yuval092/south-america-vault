# Merida

A large city, the capital of Yucatan, located about 4 hours from Cancun. Visit:
* The main avenue Paseo de Montejo with antique-designed houses.
* The central square Plaza Grande where you will find stalls on Sunday mornings, performances every night, and the Merida Cathedral.
* 78 km from the city, you will find the stunning Uxmal ruins with a giant pyramid in the middle of the jungle.
* Visit the yellow town of Izamal where all houses are painted yellow.
* Visit Merida's beaches - Progreso and Sisal.
