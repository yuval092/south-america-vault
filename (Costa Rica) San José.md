# San José

Capital of Costa Rica and the largest city. Considered the starting point for those arriving by flight. In the colorful city, you can do:
* Visit the Cathedral and National Theatre.
* La Sabana Park.
* Artisan Market.
* Namu Gallery.
* Multiplaza Mall.
* Coffee tours.
* Unique Butterfly enclosure.
* Parque Diversiones amusement park.
* Daily tour to Poás Volcano.
* Trip to 'mini jungle' in Braulio Carrillo National Park (animals, waterfalls, lagoons).
