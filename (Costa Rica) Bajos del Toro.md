# Bajos del Toro

Untouristed area located near San Ramon on the way to La Fortuna, offering an agricultural area full of farms and beautiful waterfalls. Attractions:
* Tesoro Escondido Waterfall (long hiking trail).
* Catarata del Toro (giant waterfall with circular route) and nearby Blue Falls.
* Catarata Rio Agrio farm (beautiful waterfall with hiking trail).
* Pozas Azules farm (stunning turquoise pools).
