# Villa Tunari

An exotic and peaceful town located in the heart of a rainforest, known as a place for volunteering in Bolivia. The town offers:
* Volunteering for injured animals.
* Volunteering in the community and schools.
* Volunteering with children.
* Sleeping in 'La Jungla' treehouses.
* Swimming in the river and hiking in the reserve.
