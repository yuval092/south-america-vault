# Samaipata

A peaceful and isolated town with unique and beautiful mountain scenery in central Bolivia. Attractions include:
* El Fuerte, an archaeological site on the mountain peak.
* Vallegrande - Che Guevara trail tour.
* The town zoo.
* Horseback riding.
* La Pajcha - natural water pool and waterfalls.
