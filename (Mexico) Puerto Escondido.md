# Puerto Escondido

A popular surf town in the west of the country along Mexico's Pacific coast, attracting many surfers from around the world. Its beaches stretch for 110 kilometers from Mazunte to Roca Blanca. Popular accommodation areas are La Punta and Zicatela where the largest concentration of tourists and nightlife is. In the beach town you can:
* Participate in surf camps, private surf lessons, and equipment rental.
* Trip to the calm Playa Principal.
* Visit the entertainment and restaurant center on El Adoquin street.
* Visit the most central surfer beach Playa Zicatela.
* Horseback riding trips.
* Dolphin watching and advanced fishing.
* Trip to La Reforma waterfall near the town.
* Visit bathing beaches - Playa Marinero and Puerto Angelito.
* Daily tour of the food market.
* Market tour in the pedestrian street.
* Trip to Manialtepec Lagoon where you will find bioluminescent plankton at night.
* Trip to the rocky bay Carrizalillo.
* Sea turtle rescue activity at 17:00 at Playa Bacocho.
