# Morro de São Paulo

The most famous island for travelers, a total paradise and a must-visit in Brazil. Sought after for calmness, beaches, parties, and atmosphere. Details:
* Popular beach: Second Beach (others are also good).
* Arrival: Ferry from Salvador (tough due to waves), or popular option of shuttle from airport combined with short boat ride from Valença, or flight from Salvador.
* Activities: Sailing around the island, horseback riding along beaches.
