# Paraty

An ancient colonial coastal town within a dense tropical jungle. Activities include:
* Tours to crazy waterfalls, natural rock slide, a cave inside a waterfall, high cliff jumping.
* Sailing to nearby islands in a 'Capeboat' (boat serving Caipirinhas).
* Diving in clear water with colorful fish.
* Jeep tours.
* Trek to Saco Bravo Waterfall.
* 40-minute drive to Trindade village (natural pools with especially clear water and many fish).
* Direct bus access from Rio central station. Access to Ilha Grande via taxi/bus to Angra dos Reis and then ferry.
