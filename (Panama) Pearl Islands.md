# Pearl Islands

Located south of Panama City and Isla Taboga, the cruise length is an hour and a half from Panama City port.

**Overview:**
* A group of islands with spectacular views and beautiful beaches, these are the Pacific islands of Panama
* Popular islands are Isla Contadora including 13 beautiful beaches with white sand, Isla Saboga and Isla del Rey
* The islands are known to Israelis from the reality series Survivor where 3 seasons were filmed including 'Survivor Pearl Islands'

**Activities:**
* Snorkeling
* Kayaking
* Fishing
* Whale watching and more

**Info:**
Until recently the islands were for Panama's rich and you will find pampering resorts and hotels, restaurants, golf carts with which you can reach the beaches.
