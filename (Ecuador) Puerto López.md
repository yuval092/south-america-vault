# Puerto López

Small fishing town known as the most impressive dive site in Ecuador, part of Machalilla Park. One hour from Montañita. Peaceful town offers:
* Best dive sites in country (courses, intro dives, manta ray dives).
* Snorkeling.
* Whale watching tours.
* Isla de la Plata tour.
* Tropical virgin beaches.
* Los Frailes Beach trip (white sand, clear water).
* Walking tour in Cerro San Sebastian reserve near Agua Blanca.
