# Curitiba

Located between São Paulo and Florianópolis, a modern student city. Offers:
* Rich culinary experience.
* Spectacular Botanical Garden.
* Barigui Park: A huge park with a river and green scenery where Capybaras roam free.
* Rua das Flores pedestrian street with street shows and flower beds.
* Jewish center with synagogue and pool.
* Curitiba Shopping mall.
* Handicraft market on Sundays.
* Vicente Machado nightlife street.
* Curitiba Largo da Ordem historic center (bar street).
* During Carnival (reminiscent of Rio), there is also a zombie parade.
