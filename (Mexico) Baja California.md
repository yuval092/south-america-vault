# Baja California

The northwesternmost region in the country bordering the United States (below California) and known for its abundance of nature reserves and rich attractions. In the magical peninsula, you can enjoy:
* Sailing to the famous Arch of Cabo San Lucas.
* Land border crossing to the USA.
* Trip to Balandra Beach.
* Trek in Sierra mountains including various extreme sports.
* Geyser trip at La Bufadora viewpoint.
* Ensenada street where you can enjoy shopping and restaurants.
* Sailing and watching sea lions at Isla Espiritu Santo.
* Trip to Guadalupe nature reserve.
* Horseback riding and ATV tours.
* Chill at Palmilla Beach.
* Diving with whale sharks in South Baja - La Paz.
* Watching sea turtles at Todos Santos Beach.
* Surfing.
* Visit Tijuana Beach.
* Casinos.
* Trip to Ecological Park departing Cabo.
* Fountains park in Ensenada.
* Diving with mantas and whales at Socorro Island.
* Snorkeling and sailing at Coronado Island.
* Horseback riding tours.
