# Playa Blanca

Considered one of the most beautiful beaches in Colombia. Accessible by bus, car, taxi, or boat from Cartagena (1.5 hours drive, 30 min boat). You can stay overnight and enjoy the calm area.
* Main attraction: Watching plankton (small sea creatures that glow in the dark).
