# Córdoba

The second largest city in the country offering rich nightlife.

**Highlights:**
* Visit to Alta Gracia town and Che Guevara's house
* Quebrada del Condorito National Park where you can watch condors
* Punilla Valley with spectacular mountains, streams, waterfalls and lakes
* You can visit a park shaped like a guitar (viewed from the air) called Nazca Guitar located near Rio Cuarto.
