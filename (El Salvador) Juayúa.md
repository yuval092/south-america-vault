# Juayúa

The largest town on the Route of the Flowers and you can settle in it and go out for trips from it. The most touristy town and therefore recommended as a stopover point.

**Highlights:**
* The town is full of wall paintings
* Food festival active every Saturday and Sunday between 12:00-17:00 near the main entrance
* Visit to Cristo Negro church
* Los Chorros de la Calera waterfalls
* Seven Waterfalls trek taking about 6 hours and passing through coffee plantations and tropical forest with spectacular views of volcanoes
