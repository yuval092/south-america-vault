# San Juan del Sur

A spectacular strip of beaches combined with parties until dawn, attractive surf sites for surfers, white beaches, and clear water - a true paradise. A perfect resort town and a must-visit in Nicaragua. The popular sport in town is yoga, found in every corner of the city and beaches, peace and calm in dreamy landscapes. Recommended beaches: Playa Hermosa, Maderas Beach, and Remanso Beach. You can visit the Jesus statue with a beautiful view of the area.
