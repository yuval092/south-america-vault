# Limón

Large port city serving as transit to Tortuguero, Puerto Viejo, and Cahuita National Park.
* Cahuita: Stunning nature reserve with jungle trails, beaches, animals, Jaguar/Sloth Rescue Center.
* Pacuare River rafting (considered one of the best in the world).
