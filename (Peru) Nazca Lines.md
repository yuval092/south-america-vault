# Nazca Lines

Known mainly thanks to the famous Nazca Lines (legend says aliens drew the lines). Arrival by bus from Lima, Ica or Arequipa.

**Activities:**
* Nazca Lines attraction by plane or from a tower
* Cemetery tour
* Observatory
* Giant pyramids of Cahuachi
* Sandboarding in Cerro Blanco which is the second highest dune in the world requiring 3-4 hours climb and half hour slide
