# El Chaltén

A small village sitting next to the Andes mountain range which became a central point from which the famous Carretera Austral and the popular Fitz Roy trek depart.

**Arrival:**
Arrival by bus from Calafate

**Tip:**
During peak season, book a bus exit ticket upon arrival so you don't get stuck there for a few days.

**Treks & Activities:**
* Fitz Roy Circuit trek
* Windy Pass trek
* Lago Toro trek
* Daily Tumbado trek
* Visit to Laguna Capri
* Laguna Torre and Cerro Torre
* Condors Viewpoint and Eagles Viewpoint
* Chorrillo del Salto waterfall
* Climbing and walking on glaciers (click for details)

**Info:**
Visit the tourist information stand (near the bus station) and you will receive a map with the treks there.
