# Pacaya Samiria National Reserve

Largest jungle reserve in the country located alongside famous Amazon River and in heart of rainforests also known as 'Jungle of Mirrors'.

**Activities:**
* Jungle tours - Princess/Survival
* Sailing in Amazon River
* Watching and swimming with Pink/Grey Dolphins
* Meeting local tribes
* Fishing experience
* Monkey Island
* Watching sloths, crocodiles, piranhas and very large variety of animals
