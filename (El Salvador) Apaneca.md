# Apaneca

A small and lovely town with lagoons that can be reached in short and marked treks.

**Attractions:**
* Ziplines/Canopy in the forest with one especially long section of 420 meters
* Intricate Maze attraction (Harry Potter Goblet of Fire style) - offers a fun and enjoyable experience where you can also do zipline, sky-bike, trampolines and more
* You can visit Santa Teresa hot springs - 14 hot pools at different temperatures and a spa
* From here you can visit the town Ataco
