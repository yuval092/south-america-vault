# Acapulco

A modern and attractive resort town located along the Pacific Ocean in the southwest of Mexico, including luxury hotels, hostels, apartments, and many tourist areas - the Cancun of the west side. In the vibrant and pleasant town you can enjoy:
* La Quebrada tour including amazing bays.
* Shopping at La Isla shopping center.
* Impressive viewpoint and cliff jumping into the water.
* Trip to Barra Vieja beach.
* Horseback riding trips.
* Tour of the Chapel of Peace and spectacular view of the coastline.
* SUP lessons.
* Chill at Playa Pie de la Cuesta.
* Diving and snorkeling clubs.
* Tour of Fort San Diego.
* Surfing lessons.
* Sailing in Tres Palos Lagoon with rich nature.
* Visit the Dolphinarium.
* Riviera Diamante zip-line.
