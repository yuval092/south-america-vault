# Petrópolis

A picturesque town located about an hour's drive from Rio, nestled in the mountains in a green and forested area. Attractions:
* The Summer Palace of Emperor Pedro II of Brazil (now a museum).
* Serra dos Órgãos National Park.
* Wax Museum.
* Crystal Palace.
