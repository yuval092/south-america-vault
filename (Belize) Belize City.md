# Belize City

A colorful city and the largest in Belize, also serving as a ferry crossing point to Caye Caulker. Here you will find:
* Colonial buildings, historical sites, museums, green parks, and shopping areas.
* Zip-lining attractions and cave tubing.
* Sailing and snorkeling at the magical Goff's Caye.
* The zoo, located between the City and Belmopan, featuring an impressive variety of 125 animal species.
* A visit to the Mayan site 'Altun Ha' north of Belize City, where you will find pyramids surrounded by jungle.
* A visit to the baboon and howler monkey sanctuary.
