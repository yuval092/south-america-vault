# Livingston

A town with a Jamaican style located in the east of the country along the Caribbean coast and at the end of the Rio Dulce river.

**Activities:**
* In the rural and peaceful town you can reach the Seven Altars (Siete Altares) reserve in the heart of a wild jungle
* Sailing in the beautiful Rio Dulce river
* Visit to Playa Blanca beach
* Cruise to San Felipe fortress in Rio Dulce river
* Enjoy Reggae evenings in the village
