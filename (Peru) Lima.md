# Lima

The capital city containing within it and its surroundings attractions, bars, pubs, huge parties, cafes, famous chef restaurants, parks, malls, markets, shows and football games.

**City Highlights:**
* The touristy Miraflores neighborhood and Kennedy Park (click here for more on Miraflores)
* Park of Love (Parque del Amor)
* Yitzhak Rabin Park
* Paragliding
* Cinerama cinema
* Indian markets
* Main Larco Avenue and the prestigious Larcomar mall
* Saga and Ripley shopping centers
* Plaza Real mall in San Isidro neighborhood
* The huge Jockey Plaza mall
* Walking on the boardwalk overlooking the sea and surfing (surfboards can be rented at beaches)
* Plaza de Armas in the city center and Changing of the Guard daily
* Museums
* The Blue Market (Polvos Azules) where you will find everything from clothes to electronics and souvenirs
* Thieves Market containing stolen electronics - recommended not to go there
* Gamarra Market which is the largest fabric and clothing market in South America
* Famous Magic Water Circuit (fountains and lasers show)
* Visit the ancient Barranco neighborhood
* Pachacamac archaeological site - The Lost City (click here for details)

**Trips from Lima:**
* Ica sandboarding attraction
* Swimming with sea lions
* Iquitos jungle tour in the Amazon
* Trip to mountainous Amazon Oxapampa
* Marcahuasi Stone Forest trek
* Rupac 'Paradise in the Clouds' trek
* Huancaya trek
