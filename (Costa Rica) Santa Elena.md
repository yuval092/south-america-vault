# Santa Elena

Monteverde Cloud Forest Reserve is located near the town of Santa Elena. A beautiful green nature reserve containing many water sources, rich vegetation, and fascinating forest experience. Areas and places of interest:
* Cloud Forest.
* Curi-Cancha Park.
* Zoo.
* Coffee tour in Santa Elena.
* Valle Escondido Park.
* Wooden hanging bridges walk in the forest.
* Tree Tarzan swing.
* Butterfly Farm.
* Tree climbing.
* Animal watching.
* Canopy zip-line in treetops and 'Superman'.
* Night jungle tours with climbing a particularly high Ficus tree to watch the sunset.
