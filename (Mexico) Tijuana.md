# Tijuana

A city in northwest Mexico located in Baja California district and serves as a land crossing point to the United States. In the border city you can:
* Make a refreshment stop.
* Visit Tijuana Beach.
* Stroll the lively main street Revolucion.
* Visit El Popo food and souvenir market.
* Huge Caliente Casino.
* Wrestling shows at Molema complex.
* City Cultural Center.
* At the border crossing between countries, on the US side, there is a huge outlet of all known world brands.
