# Santa Cruz

The largest and most modern city in Bolivia. It offers ample nightlife including clubs, bars, pubs, cinemas, restaurants, parks, and a zoo. Recommended places to visit:
* The central square Plaza 24 de Septiembre, home to the main cathedral with the city clock.
* San Lorenzo Church nearby.
* The main shopping center, Shopping Bolivar.
* South of the square is Santa Cruz Stadium, Cine Center cinema complex, and an ancient square called Plaza Blacutt.
* Barrio Lindo Market (clothing, footwear, electronics, furniture).
* Artecampo Market for handicrafts and art.
* Nearby ecological parks: Biocentro Guembe and Yvaga Guazu (in dense rainforests).
* Amboro National Park.
* The largest water park in Bolivia.
