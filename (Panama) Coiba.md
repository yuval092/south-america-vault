# Coiba

Coiba Island is considered the Galapagos of Panama, beaches among the most beautiful in the country, clear water in a variety of colors.

**Activities:**
* You can dive and snorkel in the island
* See special fish, sharks, reefs
* With luck also whales and giant sea turtles
