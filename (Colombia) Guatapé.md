# Guatapé

A vibrant and colorful town located about two hours drive from neighboring Medellín in the northwest of the country. In this special and colorful town, it is recommended to visit:
* The town square - Plaza de los Zócalos.
* Piedra del Peñol lagoon reserve.
* Trip to the giant rock and climbing 650 stairs.
* Giant rock viewpoint.
* Comfama Park - floating inflatables in the lagoon.
* ATV riding.
* Jet skis in the lagoon.
* Flyboard.
* Gliding.
* Speedboat cruising in the lagoon.
* Zip-lines.
* Banana boat on the lake.
* Helicopter tour over the Peñol reserve.
* Accommodation on the lake shore is an experience in itself.
