# Cabo de La Vela

A small village in northern Colombia near the Venezuelan border, known as a calm, quiet village with great weather. Main visit is for kitesurfers. Activities:
* Diving, snorkeling, kitesurfing.
* Taroa Beach.
* Desert tours.
* Sunset watching.
* Ojo del Agua beach.
* Punta Gallinas tour.
* Lighthouse viewpoint.
* Due to remoteness, food and drink are expensive. Bring cash and a small bag. Kitesurfers should bring a first aid kit.
* Arrival: From Santa Marta/Palomino to Riohacha (bus/jeep), then bus to Uribia, then taxi to Cabo (Total ~5 hours).
