# Santa Maria Huatulco

An old and calm town including a strip of peaceful beaches located on the western side of the country on the Pacific coast. In the calm beach strip you can:
* Trip to 'Magic Waterfalls' reserve.
* Snorkeling at Playa San Agustin.
* Chill at Playa La Entrega.
* ATV tours.
* Visit to Llano Grande waterfalls.
* Trip to Organo Beach.
* Fishing trips.
* Trip to Maguey Bay enclosed by two beautiful vegetation ridges.
* Cruises from Santa Cruz Bay.
* Visit the quiet Playa Bocana.
